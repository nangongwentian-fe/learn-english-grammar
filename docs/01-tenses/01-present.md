# 现在时 Present Tenses

::: tip 中文没有时态
中文说"我吃饭"、"我昨天吃饭"、"我明天吃饭"——动词"吃"从不变化，靠"昨天""明天"来表达时间。英语不一样：**动词本身会发生变化**来表达时间，这就是"时态"。现在时就是用来说"现在"相关的事情。
:::

## 1. 一般现在时 Simple Present

### 什么时候用？

一般现在时**不是**说"正在做什么"，而是说：

1. **习惯/重复的动作** — 每天做的事、经常做的事
2. **客观事实/真理** — 地球绕太阳转、水在100°C沸腾
3. **永久的状态** — 住在哪里、做什么工作

> 简单记忆：**"一直都这样"的事情，用一般现在时。**

### 怎么构成？

#### 肯定句

| 主语 | 动词形式 | 例句 |
|------|---------|------|
| I / You / We / They | 动词原形 | I **eat** breakfast at 7am. |
| He / She / It | 动词 + s/es | She **eats** breakfast at 7am. |

#### 否定句

| 主语 | 构成 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| I / You / We / They | do not (don't) + 动词原形 | I **don't eat** meat. |
| He / She / It | does not (doesn't) + 动词原形 | She **doesn't eat** meat. |

::: warning 中国人高频错误
否定句中用了 doesn't，动词就要**变回原形**！

❌ She doesn't **eats** meat.（eats 多了 s）

✅ She doesn't **eat** meat.
:::

#### 疑问句

| 主语 | 构成 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| I / You / We / They | Do + 主语 + 动词原形？ | **Do** you **like** coffee? |
| He / She / It | Does + 主语 + 动词原形？ | **Does** she **like** coffee? |

### 第三人称单数变化规则

"第三人称单数"就是 **he / she / it**（或任何可以用 he/she/it 替代的名词，如 "my mother" = she, "the cat" = it）。

| 规则 | 方法 | 举例 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般动词 | 直接加 **-s** | work → work**s**, eat → eat**s** |
| 以 s, sh, ch, x, o 结尾 | 加 **-es** | go → go**es**, watch → watch**es**, fix → fix**es** |
| 以"辅音字母 + y"结尾 | 去 y 加 **-ies** | study → stud**ies**, carry → carr**ies** |
| 以"元音字母 + y"结尾 | 直接加 **-s** | play → play**s**, enjoy → enjoy**s** |
| 特殊：have | 变为 **has** | She **has** a cat. |

::: info 什么是元音和辅音字母？
**元音字母**只有5个：a, e, i, o, u

**辅音字母**就是除了这5个以外的所有字母（b, c, d, f, g...）

例如：study 的 y 前面是 d（辅音）→ 去 y 加 ies → studies

play 的 y 前面是 a（元音）→ 直接加 s → plays
:::

### 常见时间标志词

看到这些词，大概率用一般现在时：

**every** day / week / month / year, **always**, **usually**, **often**, **sometimes**, **never**, **seldom**, on Mondays, twice a week

### 例句对比

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She go to school every day.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She <strong>goes</strong> to school every day.（She 是第三人称单数，go 须变 goes）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Water freeze at 0°C.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Water <strong>freezes</strong> at 0°C.（Water = it，第三人称单数，freeze 须变 freezes）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ He don't like math.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ He <strong>doesn't like</strong> math.（He 用 doesn't，不用 don't）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Does she likes music?</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Does she <strong>like</strong> music?（Does 已经表示第三人称，like 不再加 s）</div>

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作 Task 2** 表达普遍观点时使用一般现在时：

- *"Many people **believe** that education **is** the key to success."*（人们普遍认为…）
- *"Technology **plays** an important role in modern life."*（科技扮演…的角色）
- *"Some argue that governments **should** invest more in public health."*

**雅思口语 Part 1** 描述日常习惯：

- *"I **usually wake up** at 7 in the morning."*
- *"I **go** to the gym three times a week."*

</div>

### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="He ___ (study) English every morning."
  answer="studies"
  hint="He 是第三人称单数，study 以辅音 + y 结尾 → 去 y 加 ies → studies"
/>

<Quiz
  question="The sun ___ (rise) in the east."
  answer="rises"
  hint="这是客观事实，用一般现在时。The sun = it，第三人称单数：rise + s = rises"
/>

<Quiz
  question="They ___ (not / like) spicy food."
  answer="don't like"
  hint="They 用 do not (don't) + 动词原形：don't like"
/>

<Quiz
  question="___ your sister ___ (speak) French? (用 Does 开头)"
  answer="Does your sister speak"
  hint="your sister = she，用 Does + 主语 + 动词原形：Does your sister speak"
/>

<Quiz
  question="My mother ___ (watch) TV every evening."
  answer="watches"
  hint="My mother = she，第三人称单数。watch 以 ch 结尾 → 加 es → watches"
/>

## 2. 现在进行时 Present Continuous

### 什么时候用？

现在进行时说的是：

1. **此刻正在发生的事** — 我现在正在写作业
2. **当前这段时间持续做的事** — 我这学期在学雅思（不一定此刻在学，但这段时间一直在做）
3. **已经确定的近期计划** — 我明天要去北京（票已经买了）

> 简单记忆：**"正在进行"或"近期安排"，用现在进行时。**

### 怎么构成？

**am / is / are + 动词-ing**

| 主语 | be 动词 | 例句 |
|------|---------|------|
| I | am | I **am reading** a book. |
| He / She / It | is | She **is reading** a book. |
| We / You / They | are | They **are reading** books. |

#### 否定句：在 be 后加 not

- I **am not** (I'm not) watching TV.
- She **is not** (isn't) sleeping.
- They **are not** (aren't) working.

#### 疑问句：be 提到主语前

- **Are** you studying?（你在学习吗？）
- **Is** he sleeping?（他在睡觉吗？）

### 动词 -ing 变化规则

| 规则 | 方法 | 举例 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般动词 | 直接加 **-ing** | read → read**ing**, eat → eat**ing** |
| 以不发音的 e 结尾 | 去 e 加 **-ing** | make → mak**ing**, write → writ**ing** |
| 以"一个辅音字母"结尾且前面是"短元音" | 双写最后字母 + **-ing** | run → ru**nn**ing, sit → si**tt**ing, swim → swi**mm**ing |
| 以 ie 结尾 | ie 变 y + **-ing** | die → d**y**ing, lie → l**y**ing |

### 常见时间标志词

**now**, **right now**, **at the moment**, **currently**, **these days**, **this week/month/year**, **Look!**, **Listen!**

### 不能用进行时的动词（状态动词）

::: warning 重要
有些动词**表示状态而非动作**，通常**不能**用现在进行时：

| 类别 | 动词 |
|------|------|
| 感官/认知 | know, understand, believe, think（认为）, remember, forget |
| 情感/喜好 | like, love, hate, want, need, prefer |
| 拥有 | have（拥有）, own, belong |
| 其他 | be, seem, mean, cost |

❌ I **am knowing** the answer. → ✅ I **know** the answer.

❌ She **is liking** chocolate. → ✅ She **likes** chocolate.

❌ This book **is costing** $10. → ✅ This book **costs** $10.
:::

### 例句对比

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She is study for her IELTS exam this week.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She <strong>is studying</strong> for her IELTS exam this week.（study 需要变为 studying）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I am work now.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I <strong>am working</strong> now.（work 需要加 -ing）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ They are watch a movie.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ They <strong>are watching</strong> a movie.（watch 需要加 -ing）</div>

### 一般现在时 vs 现在进行时

| 一般现在时 | 现在进行时 |
|-----------|-----------|
| I **drink** coffee every day.（习惯：每天都喝） | I **am drinking** coffee now.（此刻正在喝） |
| She **works** at a bank.（职业：一直在银行工作） | She **is working** from home today.（今天在家办公） |
| It **rains** a lot in summer.（规律：夏天常下雨） | It **is raining** outside.（现在正在下雨） |

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思口语 Part 1** 描述当前生活状态时用现在进行时：

- *"I **am preparing** for the IELTS exam at the moment."*
- *"I **am currently studying** at Beijing University."*
- *"These days, I **am focusing** on improving my English grammar."*

**雅思口语 Part 2** 描述图片或场景时：

- *"In the picture, a man **is sitting** on a bench and **is reading** a newspaper."*

</div>

### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="Listen! The birds ___ (sing)."
  answer="are singing"
  hint="Listen! 提示此刻正在发生 → 用现在进行时：are + singing"
/>

<Quiz
  question="She ___ (write) an email right now."
  answer="is writing"
  hint="right now 表示此刻正在 → is + writing（write 去 e 加 ing）"
/>

<Quiz
  question="I ___ (not / sleep). I ___ (study)."
  answer="am not sleeping. I am studying"
  hint="否定：am not + sleeping。肯定：am + studying"
/>

<Quiz
  question="We ___ (run) in the park at the moment."
  answer="are running"
  hint="at the moment 提示正在进行 → are + running（run 双写 n 加 ing）"
/>

## 3. 现在完成时 Present Perfect

### 什么时候用？

现在完成时是中国学生觉得最难的时态之一，因为**中文里没有对应的概念**。

它表达的核心意思是：**过去发生的事，对现在有影响/有关联。**

具体场景：

1. **过去的经历**（不强调什么时候）— 我去过日本 / 我从没吃过寿司
2. **过去开始、持续到现在的事** — 她在北京住了十年（现在还住着）
3. **刚刚完成的事** — 我刚写完作业（所以现在可以休息了）
4. **到目前为止的变化/成果** — 科技已经改变了我们的生活

> 核心要点：**强调的是"结果"或"关联"，不是"什么时候做的"。**

::: warning 和一般过去时的最大区别
- **一般过去时**：强调**过去的时间点**，和现在**没关系** → I ate sushi **yesterday**.（昨天吃了，陈述过去的事）
- **现在完成时**：强调**和现在的关联**，不说具体时间 → I **have eaten** sushi **before**.（我吃过寿司 → 所以我知道味道）

❌ I **have eaten** sushi **yesterday**.（不能和具体过去时间连用！）

✅ I **ate** sushi **yesterday**.
:::

### 怎么构成？

**have / has + 过去分词（past participle）**

| 主语 | 助动词 | 例句 |
|------|--------|------|
| I / You / We / They | have | I **have finished** my homework. |
| He / She / It | has | She **has finished** her homework. |

#### 否定句

- I **have not** (haven't) **seen** that movie.
- He **has not** (hasn't) **arrived** yet.

#### 疑问句

- **Have** you **been** to Japan?（你去过日本吗？）
- **Has** she **finished** yet?（她完成了吗？）

### 过去分词怎么变？

| 类别 | 规则 | 举例 |
|------|------|------|
| 规则动词 | 和过去式一样，加 **-ed** | work → work**ed**, finish → finish**ed** |
| 不规则动词 | 需要记忆（三列表） | go → **gone**, eat → **eaten**, see → **seen**, write → **written** |

常见不规则动词表（必须记住！）：

| 原形 | 过去式 | 过去分词 | 中文 |
|------|--------|---------|------|
| go | went | **gone** | 去 |
| eat | ate | **eaten** | 吃 |
| see | saw | **seen** | 看见 |
| do | did | **done** | 做 |
| have | had | **had** | 有 |
| be | was/were | **been** | 是 |
| take | took | **taken** | 拿/花费 |
| give | gave | **given** | 给 |
| write | wrote | **written** | 写 |
| speak | spoke | **spoken** | 说 |
| make | made | **made** | 制作 |
| come | came | **come** | 来 |
| know | knew | **known** | 知道 |
| think | thought | **thought** | 认为 |
| buy | bought | **bought** | 买 |
| live | lived | **lived** | 住（规则） |
| try | tried | **tried** | 尝试（规则） |

### 常见搭配词

| 词/短语 | 用法 | 例句 |
|---------|------|------|
| **already**（已经） | 肯定句 | I have **already** finished. |
| **yet**（还没/已经…了吗） | 否定句/疑问句 | I haven't finished **yet**. / Have you finished **yet**? |
| **just**（刚刚） | 肯定句 | She has **just** left. |
| **ever**（曾经） | 疑问句 | Have you **ever** been to Paris? |
| **never**（从不） | 肯定句（含否定意义） | I have **never** tried sushi. |
| **since** + 时间点 | 从…以来 | I have lived here **since 2020**. |
| **for** + 时间段 | 持续了… | I have lived here **for 5 years**. |
| **recently / lately**（最近） | | She has been busy **recently**. |
| **so far**（到目前为止） | | **So far**, we have received 100 applications. |

::: warning since vs for — 中国人常搞混
**since** + **时间点**（具体的"从什么时候开始"）：since 2020, since Monday, since I was a child

**for** + **时间段**（"持续了多久"）：for 5 years, for two weeks, for a long time

❌ I have lived here **since** 5 years. → ✅ I have lived here **for** 5 years.

❌ I have lived here **for** 2020. → ✅ I have lived here **since** 2020.
:::

### 例句对比

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I already finished my homework.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I <strong>have already finished</strong> my homework.（强调"已完成"→ 现在完成时）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She lives here since 2020.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She <strong>has lived</strong> here since 2020.（since 表示"从过去持续到现在"→ 必须用现在完成时）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Have you ever went to Japan?</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Have you ever <strong>gone</strong> to Japan?（have 后面接过去分词 gone，不是过去式 went）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I have seen that movie yesterday.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I <strong>saw</strong> that movie yesterday.（有 yesterday 具体时间，只能用一般过去时）</div>

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作 Task 2 引言段** 常用现在完成时描述背景趋势：

- *"In recent years, the internet **has transformed** the way people access information."*
- *"Over the past decade, the number of international students **has increased** dramatically."*
- *"Technology **has made** it possible for people to work from anywhere."*

**雅思口语 Part 2** 谈经历：

- *"I **have always wanted** to visit Europe."*
- *"I **have been studying** English for three years."*（现在完成进行时，表示一直在做且还在继续）

</div>

## 三种现在时对比总结

| | 一般现在时 | 现在进行时 | 现在完成时 |
|---|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **核心意思** | 习惯/事实 | 正在做 | 过去→现在有关联 |
| **构成** | 动词原形 / 动词+s | am/is/are + doing | have/has + done |
| **时间词** | every day, always, usually | now, at the moment | already, yet, since, for |
| **例句** | I **drink** tea every day. | I **am drinking** tea now. | I **have drunk** three cups today. |
| **中文对照** | 我每天喝茶。 | 我正在喝茶。 | 我今天已经喝了三杯了。 |
