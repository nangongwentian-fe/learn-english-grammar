# 过去时 Past Tenses

::: tip 为什么要学过去时？
雅思写作 Task 1 描述图表数据时，大量使用过去时（因为图表展示的是已经发生的数据）。雅思口语 Part 2 讲述过去的经历也离不开过去时。过去时是雅思考试中使用频率最高的时态之一。
:::

## 1. 一般过去时 Simple Past

### 什么时候用？

一般过去时说的是：**过去某个时间发生的事，已经结束了，和现在没有直接关系。**

1. **过去发生并完成的动作** — 我昨天去了超市
2. **过去的状态** — 她小时候住在上海
3. **过去连续发生的一系列动作** — 我回到家，打开门，坐下来

> 简单记忆：**过去做了，现在结束了，用一般过去时。**

### 怎么构成？

#### 肯定句

主语 + 动词**过去式**（所有人称都一样，不像一般现在时需要区分第三人称！）

- I **went** to school.
- She **went** to school.（同样用 went，不需要加 s！）

#### 否定句

主语 + **did not (didn't)** + 动词**原形**

- I **didn't go** to school yesterday.

::: warning 注意
和一般现在时一样，用了 didn't，动词要**变回原形**！

❌ I didn't **went**. → ✅ I didn't **go**.
:::

#### 疑问句

**Did** + 主语 + 动词**原形**？

- **Did** you **go** to school yesterday?（动词用原形 go，不是 went）

### 动词过去式变化规则

#### 规则动词（加 -ed）

| 规则 | 方法 | 举例 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般动词 | 直接加 **-ed** | work → work**ed**, play → play**ed** |
| 以 e 结尾 | 只加 **-d** | like → like**d**, live → live**d** |
| 以"辅音 + y"结尾 | 去 y 加 **-ied** | study → stud**ied**, carry → carr**ied** |
| 以"一个辅音"结尾且前面是短元音 | 双写 + **-ed** | stop → stop**ped**, plan → plan**ned** |

#### -ed 的三种发音

| 发音 | 条件 | 举例 |
|------|------|------|
| /t/ | 动词以清辅音结尾 | work**ed**, watch**ed**, help**ed** |
| /d/ | 动词以浊辅音或元音结尾 | play**ed**, open**ed**, live**d** |
| /ɪd/ | 动词以 t 或 d 结尾 | want**ed**, need**ed**, start**ed** |

#### 不规则动词（必须记忆）

没有规律，只能背。以下是雅思中最常用的：

| 原形 | 过去式 | 中文 | 原形 | 过去式 | 中文 |
|------|--------|------|------|--------|------|
| go | **went** | 去 | get | **got** | 得到 |
| come | **came** | 来 | give | **gave** | 给 |
| see | **saw** | 看见 | take | **took** | 拿/花费 |
| eat | **ate** | 吃 | make | **made** | 制作 |
| do | **did** | 做 | say | **said** | 说 |
| have | **had** | 有 | think | **thought** | 认为 |
| know | **knew** | 知道 | buy | **bought** | 买 |
| write | **wrote** | 写 | read | **read** | 读（拼写不变，发音变） |
| speak | **spoke** | 说 | find | **found** | 发现 |
| become | **became** | 变成 | begin | **began** | 开始 |
| bring | **brought** | 带来 | build | **built** | 建造 |
| feel | **felt** | 感觉 | keep | **kept** | 保持 |
| leave | **left** | 离开 | meet | **met** | 遇见 |
| put | **put** | 放（不变） | run | **ran** | 跑 |
| sit | **sat** | 坐 | stand | **stood** | 站 |
| tell | **told** | 告诉 | win | **won** | 赢 |
| lose | **lost** | 输/丢失 | spend | **spent** | 花费 |

::: info be 动词的过去式
be 动词比较特殊：

| 主语 | 过去式 |
|------|--------|
| I / He / She / It | **was** |
| We / You / They | **were** |

否定：was not (wasn't) / were not (weren't)

疑问：**Was** she happy? / **Were** they at home?
:::

### 常见时间标志词

**yesterday**, **last** week/month/year, **ago**（two days **ago**）, **in** 2020, **when** I was young, **that day**, **at that time**

### 例句对比

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She go to London last year.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She <strong>went</strong> to London last year.（last year 是过去时间，用过去式 went）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I didn't went to the party.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I <strong>didn't go</strong> to the party.（didn't 后面用动词原形）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Did she went to school?</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>Did</strong> she <strong>go</strong> to school?（Did 后面用动词原形）</div>

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作 Task 1** 描述过去数据大量使用一般过去时：

- *"The number of students **increased** significantly between 2010 and 2015."*
- *"In 2018, approximately 5 million tourists **visited** the country."*
- *"Sales **reached** a peak of $2 million in July."*
- *"The percentage **dropped** sharply from 40% to 15%."*

**雅思口语 Part 2** 讲述过去经历：

- *"Last summer, I **went** to Yunnan with my friends. We **stayed** there for a week and **visited** many beautiful places."*

</div>

### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="They ___ (finish) the project last Monday."
  answer="finished"
  hint="last Monday 是过去具体时间 → 一般过去时：finish + ed = finished"
/>

<Quiz
  question="She ___ (not / go) to the meeting yesterday."
  answer="didn't go"
  hint="否定句：didn't + 动词原形 → didn't go（不是 didn't went）"
/>

<Quiz
  question="I ___ (be) very tired after the exam."
  answer="was"
  hint="I 用 was（be 动词的过去式）"
/>

<Quiz
  question="___ you ___ (see) the movie last night?"
  answer="Did you see"
  hint="疑问句：Did + 主语 + 动词原形 → Did you see"
/>

<Quiz
  question="He ___ (buy) a new phone two days ago."
  answer="bought"
  hint="two days ago 是过去时间。buy 的过去式是不规则变化：bought"
/>

## 2. 过去进行时 Past Continuous

### 什么时候用？

1. **过去某个时刻正在进行的动作** — 昨天晚上8点我在看电视
2. **一个动作正在进行，被另一个动作打断** — 我正在做作业的时候，电话响了
3. **两个动作同时进行** — 妈妈在做饭，爸爸在看报纸

> 简单记忆：**过去的"正在"，用过去进行时。**

### 怎么构成？

**was / were + 动词-ing**

| 主语 | 构成 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| I / He / She / It | was + doing | I **was reading** at 8pm. |
| We / You / They | were + doing | They **were sleeping** at midnight. |

否定：I **wasn't** reading. / They **weren't** sleeping.

疑问：**Were** you sleeping? / **Was** she working?

### 关键用法：when 和 while

::: info 记住这个公式
**when + 一般过去时（短动作）, 过去进行时（长动作）**

= 正在做A的时候，B突然发生了

- I **was studying** **when** she **called**.（我正在学习，这时她打来电话）
- **When** the teacher **came** in, we **were talking**.（老师进来时，我们正在说话）

**while + 过去进行时, 过去进行时**

= 两件事同时进行

- **While** I **was cooking**, my husband **was cleaning** the house.
:::

### 例句对比

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I studied when she called.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I <strong>was studying</strong> when she called.（"我正在学习"是持续动作 → 过去进行时；"她打电话"是瞬间动作 → 一般过去时）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ At 9pm last night, I watch TV.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ At 9pm last night, I <strong>was watching</strong> TV.（过去某时刻正在做 → 过去进行时）</div>

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思口语 Part 2** 讲故事时用过去进行时设置场景：

- *"It was a beautiful evening. The sun **was setting** and birds **were singing**. I **was walking** along the river when I suddenly **saw** an old friend."*

这种"过去进行时（场景）+ 一般过去时（突发事件）"的搭配在雅思口语中很加分。

</div>

### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="We ___ (have) dinner when the phone rang."
  answer="were having"
  hint="正在做的长动作 → 过去进行时：were having；rang 是打断的短动作 → 一般过去时"
/>

<Quiz
  question="At 7am this morning, I ___ (sleep)."
  answer="was sleeping"
  hint="过去某具体时刻正在做的事 → was + sleeping"
/>

<Quiz
  question="While she ___ (read), her brother ___ (play) games."
  answer="was reading"
  hint="while 连接两个同时进行的动作 → 都用过去进行时：was reading / was playing"
/>

<Quiz
  question="I ___ (walk) home when it ___ (start) to rain."
  answer="was walking"
  hint="正在走路（长动作）→ was walking；开始下雨（短动作）→ started"
/>

## 3. 过去完成时 Past Perfect

### 什么时候用？

过去完成时说的是：**过去的过去。**

当你要说两件过去的事情，需要表达哪件事**先发生**时，先发生的那件事用过去完成时。

> 时间线：过去完成时 ← 一般过去时 ← 现在
>
> She **had left**（先走了）before I **arrived**（后到的）.

**场景：**
1. **在过去某事之前已经完成的动作** — 我到的时候，她已经走了
2. **by + 过去时间点** — 到2010年为止，他已经…
3. **宾语从句中，表示"过去之前的过去"** — 她告诉我她已经完成了报告

### 怎么构成？

**had + 过去分词**（所有人称都用 had，不区分！）

- I **had finished** my homework before dinner.
- She **had already left** when I arrived.

否定：I **had not** (hadn't) finished.

疑问：**Had** you finished?

### 关键搭配词

| 搭配 | 意思 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **before** | 在…之前 | I **had eaten** before she came. |
| **after** | 在…之后 | After he **had graduated**, he found a job. |
| **by the time** | 到…的时候 | **By the time** I arrived, she **had left**. |
| **by + 过去时间** | 到…为止 | **By 2010**, the company **had grown** significantly. |
| **already** | 已经 | She **had already** heard the news. |
| **never...before** | 以前从未 | I **had never seen** snow before that winter. |

::: warning 什么时候需要用过去完成时？
**只有当你需要区分两件过去的事情的先后顺序时**，才需要过去完成时。如果只是讲一件过去的事，用一般过去时就够了。

✅ I **went** home and **watched** TV.（按顺序发生，不需要过去完成时）

✅ When I **got** home, I **realized** I **had left** my keys at the office.（三件事有先后：丢钥匙最先 → 到家 → 意识到；丢钥匙用过去完成时）
:::

### 例句对比

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She told me that she finished the report.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She told me that she <strong>had finished</strong> the report.（完成报告在"告诉我"之前 → 用过去完成时）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ By the time I arrived, the movie already started.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ By the time I arrived, the movie <strong>had already started</strong>.（电影开始在我到达之前 → 过去完成时）</div>

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作 Task 1** 描述先后顺序时用过去完成时：

- *"By 2010, the population **had already reached** 5 million."*
- *"Before the new policy was introduced, the unemployment rate **had risen** to 15%."*

**雅思写作 Task 2** 讲述过去背景：

- *"Before the internet became widespread, people **had relied** on newspapers and television for information."*

</div>

### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="By the time I arrived, the movie ___ (already / start)."
  answer="had already started"
  hint="by the time 引导 → 电影开始在我到达之前 → had + already + started"
/>

<Quiz
  question="She ___ (never / fly) before her trip to London."
  answer="had never flown"
  hint="在去伦敦之前从未坐过飞机 → had never + flown（fly → flown）"
/>

<Quiz
  question="After he ___ (finish) dinner, he went for a walk."
  answer="had finished"
  hint="先吃完饭（过去完成时）→ 再去散步（一般过去时）：had finished"
/>

<Quiz
  question="I didn't know the answer because I ___ (not / study)."
  answer="hadn't studied"
  hint="没学习发生在不知道答案之前 → had not (hadn't) + studied"
/>

## 三种过去时对比总结

| | 一般过去时 | 过去进行时 | 过去完成时 |
|---|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **核心意思** | 过去做了（已结束） | 过去正在做 | 过去的过去 |
| **构成** | 动词过去式 | was/were + doing | had + done |
| **时间词** | yesterday, last week, ago | when, while, at that time | before, after, by the time |
| **例句** | I **went** home at 6. | I **was going** home when it rained. | I **had gone** home before she called. |
| **中文对照** | 我6点回了家。 | 我正在回家的路上（下雨了）。 | 在她打电话之前我已经回家了。 |
