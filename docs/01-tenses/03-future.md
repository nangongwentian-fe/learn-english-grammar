# 将来时 Future Tenses

::: tip 英语表达将来有多种方式
中文说将来只需要加"会""要""将来"，但英语表达将来有好几种不同的方式，每种含义不同。雅思考试中需要根据语境选择正确的方式。
:::

## 1. will（一般将来时）

### 什么时候用？

**will** 用于：

1. **预测/推测未来** — 我觉得明天会下雨
2. **临时做出的决定** — 有人敲门了，我去开门（不是计划好的，而是此刻决定的）
3. **承诺/自愿做某事** — 我会帮你的、我答应不会迟到
4. **客观的未来事实** — 比赛将在下周三举行

> 简单记忆：**预测、临时决定、承诺 → 用 will**

### 怎么构成？

**will + 动词原形**（所有人称都一样！）

| 句型 | 构成 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定 | will + 动词原形 | I **will help** you.（I'll help you.） |
| 否定 | will not (won't) + 动词原形 | She **won't come** tomorrow. |
| 疑问 | Will + 主语 + 动词原形？ | **Will** you **be** there? |

::: info 缩写形式
- I **will** → I**'ll**
- You **will** → You**'ll**
- will **not** → **won't**

口语和非正式写作中经常用缩写。雅思写作中建议用完整形式。
:::

::: warning 中国人高频错误
will 后面接**动词原形**，不加 to！

❌ I will **to call** you later.

✅ I will **call** you later.

（注意：和 want to / need to 不同，will 后面不需要 to）
:::

### 例句对比

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I will to call you later.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I <strong>will call</strong> you later.（will 后面直接接动词原形，不加 to）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She wills come tomorrow.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She <strong>will come</strong> tomorrow.（will 不变形！不需要加 s）</div>

### 常见时间标志词

**tomorrow**, **next** week/month/year, **in the future**, **soon**, **I think/believe...**, **probably**, **perhaps**

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作 Task 2** 预测未来趋势（非常常用！）：

- *"It is likely that renewable energy **will replace** fossil fuels in the coming decades."*
- *"In the future, technology **will continue** to play a major role in education."*
- *"I believe that this trend **will persist** for many years to come."*

**雅思口语 Part 3** 讨论未来变化：

- *"I think more people **will work** from home in the future."*
- *"The government **will probably need** to invest more in public transportation."*

</div>

### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="Don't worry, I ___ (help) you with that."
  answer="will help"
  hint="自愿帮忙（临时决定/承诺）→ will + 动词原形：will help"
/>

<Quiz
  question="I think it ___ (rain) tomorrow."
  answer="will rain"
  hint="I think 引导的预测 → will + 动词原形：will rain"
/>

<Quiz
  question="She ___ (not / come) to the party tonight."
  answer="won't come"
  hint="否定：will not (won't) + 动词原形 → won't come"
/>

<Quiz
  question="___ you ___ (be) at home this evening?"
  answer="Will you be"
  hint="疑问句：Will + 主语 + 动词原形 → Will you be"
/>

## 2. be going to

### 什么时候用？

**be going to** 用于：

1. **已经计划好的事** — 我打算明年出国留学（已经决定了，不是此刻才决定）
2. **基于现有证据的预测** — 看那些乌云，要下雨了（有证据支撑的判断）

> 简单记忆：**有计划、有证据 → 用 be going to**

### will vs be going to

这是雅思考试中最容易混淆的一对，区别如下：

| | will | be going to |
|---|------|------------|
| **决定的时间** | 说话时此刻决定的 | 说话前已经决定好的 |
| **预测的依据** | 个人观点/感觉 | 有具体证据 |
| **例子** | （电话响了）I**'ll answer** it.（此刻决定） | I**'m going to visit** my parents this weekend.（早就计划好了） |
| **预测例子** | I think it **will** rain tomorrow.（我觉得） | Look at those clouds. It**'s going to** rain.（看到乌云了） |

::: info 记忆技巧
- **will** = 脑子里突然想到的 / 纯猜测
- **be going to** = 早就想好的 / 有迹象的
:::

### 怎么构成？

**am / is / are + going to + 动词原形**

| 主语 | 构成 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| I | am going to | I **am going to study** abroad next year. |
| He / She / It | is going to | She **is going to take** the IELTS exam. |
| We / You / They | are going to | They **are going to move** to a new city. |

否定：I **am not going to** give up. / She **isn't going to** come.

疑问：**Are** you **going to** attend the meeting?

### 例句对比

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She will going to study abroad next year.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She <strong>is going to study</strong> abroad next year.（be going to 需要 be 动词 + going to + 动词原形，不能和 will 混用）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I going to buy a new car.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I <strong>am going to</strong> buy a new car.（不能省略 am！）</div>

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思口语 Part 1/3** 谈论计划：

- *"I **am going to take** the IELTS exam next month. I have already registered."*
- *"After graduation, I **am going to look for** a job in the technology industry."*

**写作中一般用 will 多于 be going to**，因为 be going to 比较口语化。

</div>

### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="Look at those clouds. It ___ (rain)."
  answer="is going to rain"
  hint="有证据（乌云）的预测 → be going to：is going to rain"
/>

<Quiz
  question="She ___ (travel) to Japan next summer. She already bought the tickets."
  answer="is going to travel"
  hint="已经计划好了（票都买了）→ is going to travel"
/>

<Quiz
  question="We ___ (not / move) to a new house. We decided to stay."
  answer="are not going to move"
  hint="已经做出的决定 → are not going to move（或 aren't going to move）"
/>

## 3. 现在进行时表将来

### 什么时候用？

你没看错，**现在进行时**也可以表示将来！但只用于：

**已经确定的、做好安排的近期计划**（通常涉及具体的时间和地点）

> be going to 是"打算/计划做"，现在进行时表将来是"已经安排好了"（更确定）

### 例句

- I **am meeting** my friend at 3pm tomorrow.（已经约好了，3点见面）
- We **are flying** to Shanghai on Friday.（机票已经买了）
- She **is starting** a new job next Monday.（入职日期已确定）

### 和 be going to 的区别

| be going to | 现在进行时 |
|------------|-----------|
| 计划/打算做（可能还没有具体安排） | 已经安排好了（有具体时间地点） |
| I **am going to visit** my parents.（打算去） | I **am visiting** my parents this Sunday.（已经说好了周日去） |

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思口语 Part 1** 谈近期安排：

- *"I **am taking** the IELTS exam next Saturday."*（已经报了名）
- *"My family and I **are going** on holiday next week."*（已经安排好了）

</div>

### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="We ___ (have) a meeting at 2pm tomorrow. It's already scheduled."
  answer="are having"
  hint="已经安排好的具体计划 → 现在进行时表将来：are having"
/>

## 4. 将来完成时 Future Perfect

### 什么时候用？

表示**在将来某个时间点之前，某事将已经完成**。

> 关键词：**by + 将来时间**（到…时候为止）

- **By** next June, I **will have finished** all my IELTS preparation.（到明年六月，我将已经完成所有雅思准备）
- **By** 2050, scientists **will have developed** new energy sources.（到2050年，科学家将已经开发出…）

### 怎么构成？

**will have + 过去分词**

| 句型 | 构成 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定 | will have + done | I **will have graduated** by then. |
| 否定 | will not have + done | She **won't have arrived** by 6pm. |
| 疑问 | Will + 主语 + have + done? | **Will** you **have finished** by Friday? |

### 和一般将来时的区别

| 一般将来时 | 将来完成时 |
|-----------|-----------|
| I **will finish** my homework.（我会完成） | I **will have finished** my homework **by 8pm**.（到8点我将已经完成了） |
| 只是说"会做" | 强调"在某个时间点之前完成" |

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作 Task 1** 预测将来数据（搭配 by + 将来时间）：

- *"By 2050, the global temperature **will have risen** by 2 degrees."*
- *"By the end of the century, the world population **will have reached** 10 billion."*

**雅思写作 Task 2** 展望长远变化：

- *"By the time today's children grow up, technology **will have changed** the workplace beyond recognition."*

</div>

### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="By the time you arrive, I ___ (finish) cooking."
  answer="will have finished"
  hint="by the time 引导将来时间点 → 在那之前完成 → will have + finished"
/>

<Quiz
  question="By next year, she ___ (work) here for ten years."
  answer="will have worked"
  hint="到明年为止工作了十年 → will have + worked"
/>

<Quiz
  question="By 2030, many countries ___ (ban) plastic bags."
  answer="will have banned"
  hint="到2030年之前将已经禁止 → will have + banned（ban 双写 n + ed）"
/>

## 四种将来表达对比总结

| | will | be going to | 现在进行时 | 将来完成时 |
|---|------|------------|-----------|-----------|
| **核心意思** | 预测/临时决定/承诺 | 已有计划/有证据的预测 | 已安排好的近期计划 | 将来某时前已完成 |
| **构成** | will + do | am/is/are going to + do | am/is/are + doing | will have + done |
| **正式程度** | 写作口语都常用 | 偏口语 | 偏口语 | 偏正式/写作 |
| **例句** | I **will help** you. | I **am going to study** tonight. | I **am meeting** her at 3pm. | I **will have finished** by 8pm. |
| **中文** | 我会帮你。 | 我今晚打算学习。 | 我3点和她见面。 | 到8点我将完成。 |

::: warning 雅思考试建议
- **写作**：多用 **will** 和 **will have**（正式），少用 be going to（偏口语）
- **口语**：四种都用，展示语法多样性 → 加分
- **Task 1 预测趋势**：用 **will + 动词** 或 **is expected to + 动词**
- **Task 2 讨论未来**：用 **will** 为主，搭配 **is likely to** / **is expected to** 等表达
:::
