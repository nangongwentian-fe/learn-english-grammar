# 可数与不可数名词 Countable & Uncountable Nouns

::: tip 中文没有"可数/不可数"的概念！
中文说"一个建议"、"两条信息"、"三件家具"，用"量词"（个、条、件）来数所有东西。但英语没有量词系统，它把名词分成两大类：**可数名词**（Countable Nouns）可以直接加数字，**不可数名词**（Uncountable Nouns）不能直接加数字。这个区别直接影响冠词（a/an/the）、动词（is/are）和数量词（many/much）的使用，是中国学生的高频错误区。
:::

---

## 1. 可数名词 Countable Nouns

### 什么是可数名词？

可数名词就是**可以一个一个数**的东西：one book, two books, three books...

可数名词有两种形式：
- **单数**（singular）：一个 → a book / one book
- **复数**（plural）：两个及以上 → two books / many books

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**可数名词单数不能"裸奔"！** 前面必须有 a/an, the, my, this 等修饰词。

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I need pen.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I need <strong>a</strong> pen. / I need <strong>the</strong> pen. / I need <strong>my</strong> pen.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She bought car last week.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She bought <strong>a</strong> car last week.</div>

中文说"我需要笔"、"她买了车"完全正常，但英语的可数名词单数**永远不能独立存在**！
:::

---

## 2. 可数名词的复数变化规则

### 规则变化

| 规则 | 方法 | 举例 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般名词 | 直接加 **-s** | book → book**s**, cat → cat**s**, student → student**s** |
| 以 s, sh, ch, x, z 结尾 | 加 **-es** | bus → bus**es**, dish → dish**es**, watch → watch**es**, box → box**es** |
| 以"辅音字母 + y"结尾 | 去 y 加 **-ies** | city → cit**ies**, baby → bab**ies**, story → stor**ies**, country → countr**ies** |
| 以"元音字母 + y"结尾 | 直接加 **-s** | boy → boy**s**, day → day**s**, key → key**s**, toy → toy**s** |
| 以 f 或 fe 结尾 | 去 f/fe 加 **-ves** | knife → kni**ves**, wife → wi**ves**, life → li**ves**, leaf → lea**ves**, half → hal**ves** |
| 以 f 结尾（例外） | 直接加 **-s** | roof → roof**s**, belief → belief**s**, chief → chief**s** |
| 以"辅音字母 + o"结尾 | 多数加 **-es** | potato → potato**es**, tomato → tomato**es**, hero → hero**es** |
| 以"元音字母 + o"结尾 | 直接加 **-s** | zoo → zoo**s**, radio → radio**s**, video → video**s** |
| 以 o 结尾（外来词/缩写） | 直接加 **-s** | photo → photo**s**, piano → piano**s**, memo → memo**s** |

::: info 关于 -o 结尾的记忆技巧
需要加 -es 的常见词其实就那么几个，记住它们就行：
- **potato**es, **tomato**es, **hero**es, **echo**es, **veto**es

其他大部分 -o 结尾的词直接加 -s。
:::

### 不规则变化（必须背！）

| 单数 | 复数 | 中文 | 记忆方法 |
|------|------|------|---------|
| man | **men** | 男人 | a → e |
| woman | **women** | 女人 | a → e（读音也变：/ˈwʊmən/ → /ˈwɪmɪn/） |
| child | **children** | 孩子 | 加 -ren |
| tooth | **teeth** | 牙齿 | oo → ee |
| foot | **feet** | 脚 | oo → ee |
| goose | **geese** | 鹅 | oo → ee |
| mouse | **mice** | 老鼠 | ouse → ice |
| person | **people** | 人（们） | 完全不同的词 |
| ox | **oxen** | 公牛 | 加 -en |

### 单复数同形（形式不变）

| 单词 | 例句 | 中文 |
|------|------|------|
| sheep | one sheep → two **sheep** | 绵羊 |
| fish | one fish → two **fish** | 鱼（指种类时可用 fishes） |
| deer | one deer → two **deer** | 鹿 |
| aircraft | one aircraft → two **aircraft** | 飞机 |
| series | one series → two **series** | 系列 |
| species | one species → two **species** | 物种 |
| means | one means → two **means** | 方式/手段 |

<div class="example-wrong">❌ There are many sheeps on the farm.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ There are many <strong>sheep</strong> on the farm.（sheep 单复数同形，不加 s）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Two childs are playing in the garden.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Two <strong>children</strong> are playing in the garden.（child 的复数是 children）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I brush my tooths twice a day.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I brush my <strong>teeth</strong> twice a day.（tooth 的复数是 teeth）</div>

### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="There are three ___ (child) in the family."
  answer="children"
  hint="child 的复数是不规则变化 → children"
/>

<Quiz
  question="She bought two ___ (knife) from the store."
  answer="knives"
  hint="knife 以 fe 结尾 → 去 fe 加 ves → knives"
/>

<Quiz
  question="The farmer has fifty ___ (sheep)."
  answer="sheep"
  hint="sheep 单复数同形，不需要变化 → sheep"
/>

<Quiz
  question="There are many ___ (city) in China."
  answer="cities"
  hint="city 以辅音 + y 结尾 → 去 y 加 ies → cities"
/>

<Quiz
  question="He ate three ___ (potato) for dinner."
  answer="potatoes"
  hint="potato 以辅音 + o 结尾 → 加 es → potatoes"
/>

## 3. 不可数名词 Uncountable Nouns

### 什么是不可数名词？

不可数名词是**不能一个一个数**的东西。你不能说 "one water, two waters" 或 "one information, two informations"。

不可数名词有以下特征：
- **没有复数形式**（不能加 -s）
- **不能用 a/an**（因为不能说"一个"）
- **动词用单数形式**（is, has, was 等）

### 常见不可数名词分类表

| 类别 | 不可数名词 | 中文 |
|------|-----------|------|
| **信息/建议类** | information, advice, news, knowledge, evidence, research | 信息、建议、新闻、知识、证据、研究 |
| **家具/设备类** | furniture, equipment, luggage/baggage, machinery | 家具、设备、行李、机械 |
| **食物/饮料（泛指）** | water, milk, rice, bread, meat, fruit, food, sugar, salt | 水、牛奶、米饭、面包、肉、水果、食物、糖、盐 |
| **材料** | paper, wood, glass, gold, iron, cotton, plastic | 纸、木材、玻璃、金、铁、棉、塑料 |
| **抽象概念** | education, health, happiness, love, music, art, work, progress, traffic, weather | 教育、健康、幸福、爱、音乐、艺术、工作、进步、交通、天气 |
| **学科** | mathematics, physics, economics, politics | 数学、物理、经济学、政治学 |
| **自然现象** | rain, snow, thunder, sunshine, electricity | 雨、雪、雷、阳光、电 |
| **其他常见** | homework, housework, money, accommodation, behaviour, damage, permission, transport, rubbish/garbage | 作业、家务、钱、住处、行为、损害、许可、运输、垃圾 |

::: warning 中国人高频错误
以下是中国学生最常犯的不可数名词错误——因为中文里这些词都可以"数"：

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 | 中文直译的干扰 |
|---------|--------|---------------|
| ❌ **informations** | ✅ information | 中文说"几条信息" |
| ❌ **an advice** / **advices** | ✅ advice / some advice | 中文说"一个建议" |
| ❌ **furnitures** | ✅ furniture | 中文说"几件家具" |
| ❌ **equipments** | ✅ equipment | 中文说"几台设备" |
| ❌ **knowledges** | ✅ knowledge | 中文说"各种知识" |
| ❌ **homeworks** | ✅ homework | 中文说"几份作业" |
| ❌ **luggages** | ✅ luggage | 中文说"几件行李" |
| ❌ **a news** | ✅ news / a piece of news | 中文说"一条新闻" |
| ❌ **progresses** | ✅ progress | 中文说"取得了几项进步" |
| ❌ **an evidence** | ✅ evidence / a piece of evidence | 中文说"一条证据" |
| ❌ **damages** | ✅ damage（作"损害"义时） | 中文说"造成了损害" |
| ❌ **a research** | ✅ research / a research study | 中文说"一项研究" |

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She gave me some informations about the course.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She gave me some <strong>information</strong> about the course.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Can you give me an advice?</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Can you give me some <strong>advice</strong>? / Can you give me <strong>a piece of</strong> advice?</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ We need to buy new furnitures for the office.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ We need to buy new <strong>furniture</strong> for the office.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I have a lot of homeworks to do tonight.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I have a lot of <strong>homework</strong> to do tonight.</div>
:::

### 不可数名词的动词搭配

不可数名词永远是**单数**，所以动词也用**单数形式**：

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|--------|
| ❌ The furniture **are** new. | ✅ The furniture **is** new. |
| ❌ The information **were** useful. | ✅ The information **was** useful. |
| ❌ The news **are** shocking. | ✅ The news **is** shocking.（news 虽然有 s，但它是不可数！） |

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**news 看起来像复数（有 -s），但它是不可数名词，动词用单数！**

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The news are very exciting.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The news <strong>is</strong> very exciting.</div>

同理：mathematics, physics, economics, politics 这些学科名词也是单数：
- Mathematics **is** my favourite subject.（不是 are）
:::

## 4. 如何"数"不可数名词

虽然不可数名词不能直接加数字，但我们可以用**量词短语**来"数"它们：

### 常用量词表

| 量词 | 适用的不可数名词 | 例句 |
|------|-----------------|------|
| **a piece of** | advice, information, news, furniture, evidence, paper, music, work | **a piece of** advice（一条建议） |
| **a glass of** | water, milk, juice, wine, beer | **a glass of** water（一杯水） |
| **a cup of** | tea, coffee, water | **a cup of** tea（一杯茶） |
| **a bottle of** | water, milk, wine, beer, juice | **a bottle of** water（一瓶水） |
| **a slice of** | bread, pizza, cake, cheese | **a slice of** bread（一片面包） |
| **a loaf of** | bread | **a loaf of** bread（一条面包） |
| **a bowl of** | rice, soup, noodles | **a bowl of** rice（一碗米饭） |
| **a bar of** | chocolate, soap, gold | **a bar of** chocolate（一块巧克力） |
| **a sheet of** | paper | **a sheet of** paper（一张纸） |
| **a grain of** | rice, sand, salt, sugar | **a grain of** rice（一粒米） |
| **a drop of** | water, blood, rain | **a drop of** water（一滴水） |
| **an item of** | furniture, clothing, equipment, news | **an item of** furniture（一件家具） |
| **a set of** | furniture, equipment | **a set of** furniture（一套家具） |
| **a tube of** | toothpaste | **a tube of** toothpaste（一管牙膏） |

### 量词复数

要表示"几个"时，**让量词变复数**，不是让不可数名词变复数：

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|--------|
| ❌ two advices | ✅ two **pieces** of advice |
| ❌ three informations | ✅ three **pieces** of information |
| ❌ two furnitures | ✅ two **pieces/items** of furniture |
| ❌ two breads | ✅ two **slices/loaves** of bread |
| ❌ three waters | ✅ three **glasses/bottles** of water |

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I need two advices from you.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I need two <strong>pieces of advice</strong> from you.（advice 不可数，用 pieces of 来数）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Can I have two waters?</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Can I have two <strong>glasses of water</strong>?（water 不可数，用 glasses of 来数）</div>

### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="Can you give me a ___ of advice? (一条建议)"
  answer="piece"
  hint="advice 不可数，用 a piece of advice"
/>

<Quiz
  question="I'd like two ___ of bread, please. (两片面包)"
  answer="slices"
  hint="bread 不可数，用 slices of bread"
/>

<Quiz
  question="She drank three ___ of water. (三杯水)"
  answer="glasses"
  hint="water 不可数，用 glasses of water"
/>

## 5. 既可数又不可数的名词

有些名词**既可以是可数的，也可以是不可数的**，但**意思不同**！

| 名词 | 不可数（泛指/抽象） | 可数（具体/种类） |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| **paper** | 纸（材料）：I need some **paper**. | 报纸/论文：I read two **papers** today. |
| **glass** | 玻璃（材料）：The table is made of **glass**. | 玻璃杯：I broke two **glasses**. |
| **experience** | 经验（抽象）：She has a lot of **experience**. | 经历（具体事件）：It was an amazing **experience**. |
| **time** | 时间（抽象）：I don't have enough **time**. | 次数：I've been there three **times**. |
| **work** | 工作（抽象）：I have too much **work**. | 作品：The museum has many famous **works**. |
| **room** | 空间（抽象）：There's no **room** for more people. | 房间：The hotel has 200 **rooms**. |
| **light** | 光线（抽象）：There isn't enough **light**. | 灯：Please turn off the **lights**. |
| **chicken** | 鸡肉（食物）：I like **chicken**. | 鸡（活的）：There are three **chickens** in the yard. |
| **coffee** | 咖啡（饮料）：I love **coffee**. | 一杯咖啡：Two **coffees**, please.（口语中常见） |
| **fruit** | 水果（泛指）：**Fruit** is healthy. | 水果种类：Tropical **fruits** include mangoes and papayas. |
| **hair** | 头发（整体）：She has long **hair**. | 几根头发：I found two **hairs** in my soup. |

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She has many experiences in teaching.（想说"教学经验丰富"）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She has a lot of <strong>experience</strong> in teaching.（泛指经验，不可数）</div>

<div class="example-right">✅ Living abroad was a wonderful <strong>experience</strong>.（一次具体的经历，可数）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I don't have enough times to finish.（想说"没有足够的时间"）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I don't have enough <strong>time</strong> to finish.（泛指时间，不可数）</div>

<div class="example-right">✅ I've called you three <strong>times</strong>.（三次，可数）</div>

## 6. 集合名词 Collective Nouns

集合名词指的是**一组人或事物**，有些特殊规则：

| 名词 | 作单数（强调整体） | 作复数（强调个体成员） |
|------|-------------------|---------------------|
| family | My family **is** big.（我家很大） | My family **are** all doctors.（我家人都是医生） |
| team | The team **is** winning.（这个队在赢） | The team **are** wearing their new uniforms.（队员们穿着新制服） |
| government | The government **is** planning a new policy.（政府在计划） | The government **are** divided on this issue.（政府成员有分歧） |
| police | — | The police **are** looking for the thief.（police 永远用复数！） |
| people | — | **People are** kind.（people 永远用复数！person 是单数） |
| staff | The staff **is** well-trained.（整体） | The staff **are** friendly.（个体） |

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**police 和 people 永远用复数动词！**

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The police is coming.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The police <strong>are</strong> coming.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ People is friendly here.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ People <strong>are</strong> friendly here.</div>

注意：person 是单数，people 是它的复数形式。如果想说"一个人"，用 **a person** 或 **one person**。
:::

## 7. 可数与不可数名词完整对比

| | 可数名词 | 不可数名词 |
|---|---------|-----------|
| **能不能加 -s？** | ✅ 可以：books, students | ❌ 不可以：~~informations~~ |
| **能不能用 a/an？** | ✅ 可以：a book, an idea | ❌ 不可以：~~an advice~~ |
| **能不能用数字？** | ✅ 可以：two books | ❌ 不可以：~~two furnitures~~（要说 two pieces of furniture） |
| **用 many 还是 much？** | many books | much water |
| **用 few 还是 little？** | few books | little water |
| **用 a number of 还是 an amount of？** | a number of books | an amount of water |
| **动词单复数** | 看单复数：A book **is**... / Books **are**... | 永远单数：Water **is**... |

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作中不可数名词的正确使用：**

以下是雅思写作高频不可数名词，确保你不会加 -s：

- *"The government should provide more **information** about..."*（不是 informations）
- *"Students can gain practical **experience** from..."*（不是 experiences，除非指多次具体经历）
- *"This **evidence** suggests that..."*（不是 evidences）
- *"Experts have conducted extensive **research** on..."*（不是 researches）
- *"**Traffic** congestion is a serious problem."*（traffic 不可数）
- *"The **accommodation** provided by the university is..."*（不是 accommodations，英式英语中不可数）
- *"People's **behaviour** has changed significantly."*（不是 behaviours，泛指行为方式时不可数）
- *"Significant **progress** has been made in..."*（不是 progresses）

</div>
