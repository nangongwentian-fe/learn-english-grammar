# 基本被动语态 Basic Passive

::: tip 被动语态的核心概念
中文里，"我吃了苹果" 和 "苹果**被**我吃了" 说的是同一件事，但**视角不同**：第一句关注"我"（做动作的人），第二句关注"苹果"（被做的对象）。

英语也一样：
- **主动语态 (Active)**：Someone **eats** the apple.（某人吃苹果 → 关注"某人"）
- **被动语态 (Passive)**：The apple **is eaten**.（苹果被吃了 → 关注"苹果"）

**什么时候用被动？** 当我们更关心**动作本身**或**承受者**，而不太关心是谁做的。
:::

---

## 1. 主动语态 vs 被动语态

### 概念对比

| | 主动语态 Active | 被动语态 Passive |
|---|---|---|
| **主语是** | 动作的**执行者**（做动作的人/物） | 动作的**承受者**（被做的对象） |
| **关注点** | **谁**做了这件事 | 这件事**被做了** |
| **例句** | Tom **cleaned** the room. | The room **was cleaned** (by Tom). |
| **中文对照** | 汤姆打扫了房间。 | 房间**被**（汤姆）打扫了。 |

### 句子结构变化

主动句改成被动句，核心变化是**主语和宾语互换位置**：

```
主动：Tom       cleaned    the room.
      ↓(执行者)   ↓(动词)    ↓(承受者)

被动：The room   was cleaned   by Tom.
      ↓(承受者→变主语)  ↓(be+过去分词)  ↓(执行者→变by短语)
```

> 简单记忆：**原来的宾语提到前面当主语，动词变成 "be + 过去分词"，原来的主语放到 by 后面（常省略）。**

### 更多例句

| 主动语态 | 被动语态 |
|---------|---------|
| People **speak** English worldwide. | English **is spoken** worldwide. |
| 全世界的人说英语。 | 英语在全世界被说（被使用）。 |
| Shakespeare **wrote** Hamlet. | Hamlet **was written** by Shakespeare. |
| 莎士比亚写了《哈姆雷特》。 | 《哈姆雷特》是莎士比亚写的。 |
| They **will build** a new hospital. | A new hospital **will be built**. |
| 他们将建一所新医院。 | 一所新医院将被建造。 |

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**不是所有动词都能变被动！** 只有**及物动词**（后面能接宾语的动词）才能变被动。

**不及物动词**（不接宾语的动词）不能变被动：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The accident was happened yesterday.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The accident <strong>happened</strong> yesterday.（happen 是不及物动词，不能用被动）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The population was increased.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The population <strong>increased</strong>.（increase 在这里是不及物动词，"人口增长了"）</div>

常见**不能用被动**的不及物动词：happen, occur, appear, disappear, exist, rise, fall, arrive, die, sleep, come, go
:::

## 2. 被动语态的构成

### 核心公式

> **be + 过去分词 (past participle)**

这是被动语态**唯一**的核心结构。不管什么时态，被动语态都是用这个公式，只是 **be 动词的形式随时态变化**。

### 过去分词回顾

过去分词就是动词的"第三列"形式（详见时态章节）：

| 类别 | 规则 | 举例 |
|------|------|------|
| 规则动词 | 动词 + **-ed** | clean → clean**ed**, use → us**ed**, open → open**ed** |
| 不规则动词 | 需要记忆 | write → **written**, make → **made**, build → **built**, speak → **spoken** |

常见不规则过去分词（被动语态高频词）：

| 原形 | 过去分词 | 中文 | 被动语态例句 |
|------|---------|------|------------|
| make | **made** | 制作 | This product is **made** in China. |
| build | **built** | 建造 | The bridge was **built** in 2005. |
| write | **written** | 写 | The book was **written** by a famous author. |
| speak | **spoken** | 说 | English is **spoken** in many countries. |
| give | **given** | 给 | He was **given** a prize. |
| take | **taken** | 拿/带 | The photo was **taken** last year. |
| know | **known** | 知道 | She is **known** for her kindness. |
| see | **seen** | 看见 | The movie has been **seen** by millions. |
| do | **done** | 做 | The work must be **done** by Friday. |
| eat | **eaten** | 吃 | The cake was **eaten** by the children. |
| find | **found** | 发现 | The answer was **found** quickly. |
| sell | **sold** | 卖 | Over 1 million copies were **sold**. |
| teach | **taught** | 教 | English is **taught** in most schools. |
| hold | **held** | 举办/握 | The meeting was **held** yesterday. |
| send | **sent** | 发送 | The email was **sent** to all employees. |
| show | **shown** | 展示 | The results are **shown** in Table 1. |
| pay | **paid** | 支付 | Workers should be **paid** fairly. |
| cut | **cut** | 切 | The ribbon was **cut** by the mayor. |

## 3. 各时态的被动语态

这是本节的**核心内容**。每个时态的被动语态，都是把 be 动词变成对应时态的形式 + 过去分词。

### 总览表

| 时态 | 主动语态 | 被动语态 | be 的形式 |
|------|---------|---------|----------|
| 一般现在时 | They **clean** the room. | The room **is cleaned**. | am / is / are |
| 一般过去时 | They **cleaned** the room. | The room **was cleaned**. | was / were |
| 一般将来时 | They **will clean** the room. | The room **will be cleaned**. | will be |
| 现在进行时 | They **are cleaning** the room. | The room **is being cleaned**. | am / is / are + being |
| 过去进行时 | They **were cleaning** the room. | The room **was being cleaned**. | was / were + being |
| 现在完成时 | They **have cleaned** the room. | The room **has been cleaned**. | have / has + been |
| 过去完成时 | They **had cleaned** the room. | The room **had been cleaned**. | had been |
| 将来完成时 | They **will have cleaned** the room. | The room **will have been cleaned**. | will have been |

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**被动语态中最常犯的错误是漏掉 be 动词！**

中文的"被"字句不需要额外的动词变化，但英语被动必须有 **be**：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The room cleaned every day.（漏了 be 动词）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The room <strong>is cleaned</strong> every day.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The letter written yesterday.（漏了 be 动词）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The letter <strong>was written</strong> yesterday.</div>
:::

### 3.1 一般现在时被动

**构成：am / is / are + 过去分词**

用于描述**经常发生的事**、**规律**、**事实**——以被动视角表达。

#### 肯定句

| 主语 | be | 过去分词 | 例句 |
|------|-----|---------|------|
| I | am | | I **am called** Jack.（我叫杰克。） |
| He / She / It | is | + 过去分词 | English **is spoken** in the UK.（英国说英语。） |
| We / You / They | are | | Cars **are made** in this factory.（汽车在这个工厂被制造。） |

#### 否定句：be + not + 过去分词

- Meat **is not (isn't) sold** in this shop.（这家店不卖肉。）
- These books **are not (aren't) written** in Chinese.（这些书不是用中文写的。）

#### 疑问句：be 提到主语前

- **Is** English **taught** at your school?（你们学校教英语吗？）
- **Are** these products **made** in Japan?（这些产品是日本制造的吗？）

#### 例句对比

<div class="example-wrong">❌ English is speak in many countries.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ English is <strong>spoken</strong> in many countries.（speak 的过去分词是 spoken，不是 speak）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Rice are grown in China.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Rice <strong>is</strong> grown in China.（Rice 是不可数名词，用 is）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Many languages are spoke in India.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Many languages are <strong>spoken</strong> in India.（spoke 是过去式，过去分词是 spoken）</div>

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作 Task 1**（流程图）大量使用一般现在时被动：

- *"First, the raw materials **are collected** from the fields."*（首先，原材料从田地里被收集。）
- *"The water **is filtered** and then **is stored** in tanks."*（水被过滤，然后被储存在罐子里。）
- *"Finally, the finished product **is packaged** and **is delivered** to shops."*（最终，成品被包装并被配送到商店。）

</div>

#### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="English ___ (teach) in most schools around the world."
  answer="is taught"
  hint="English = 单数，用 is + 过去分词。teach 的过去分词是 taught"
/>

<Quiz
  question="These cars ___ (not / make) in Germany."
  answer="are not made"
  hint="These cars = 复数，用 are + not + 过去分词。make 的过去分词是 made"
/>

### 3.2 一般过去时被动

**构成：was / were + 过去分词**

用于描述**过去发生的事**——以被动视角表达。

#### 肯定句

| 主语 | be | 例句 |
|------|-----|------|
| I / He / She / It | was + 过去分词 | The window **was broken** yesterday.（窗户昨天被打碎了。） |
| We / You / They | were + 过去分词 | Many people **were injured** in the accident.（很多人在事故中受伤。） |

#### 否定句：was/were + not + 过去分词

- The email **was not (wasn't) sent** on time.（邮件没有按时发送。）
- The students **were not (weren't) told** about the change.（学生们没有被告知这个变化。）

#### 疑问句：was/were 提到主语前

- **Was** the bridge **built** in the 19th century?（这座桥是19世纪建的吗？）
- **Were** the results **published** last week?（结果上周公布了吗？）

#### 例句对比

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The building was build in 1990.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The building was <strong>built</strong> in 1990.（build 的过去分词是 built，不是 build）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The letters was sent yesterday.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The letters <strong>were</strong> sent yesterday.（letters 是复数，用 were）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The meeting was hold last Monday.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The meeting was <strong>held</strong> last Monday.（hold 的过去分词是 held，不是 hold）</div>

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作 Task 1**（图表题）描述过去的数据常用一般过去时被动：

- *"A sharp increase **was observed** between 2000 and 2010."*（2000年至2010年间观察到了急剧增长。）
- *"The highest figure **was recorded** in 2015."*（最高数值记录于2015年。）
- *"The survey **was conducted** among 1,000 participants."*（这项调查在1000名参与者中进行。）

</div>

#### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="The Eiffel Tower ___ (build) in 1889."
  answer="was built"
  hint="The Eiffel Tower = 单数，过去时间 1889 → was + built"
/>

<Quiz
  question="Many houses ___ (destroy) in the earthquake last year."
  answer="were destroyed"
  hint="Many houses = 复数 → were + destroyed"
/>

### 3.3 一般将来时被动

**构成：will be + 过去分词**

用于描述**将来会发生的事**——以被动视角表达。

#### 肯定句

- The new school **will be opened** next month.（新学校下个月将被开放。）
- The results **will be announced** tomorrow.（结果明天将被公布。）

#### 否定句：will not (won't) be + 过去分词

- The meeting **will not (won't) be held** on Friday.（会议不会在周五举行。）
- Phones **won't be allowed** during the exam.（考试期间不允许使用手机。）

#### 疑问句：Will + 主语 + be + 过去分词？

- **Will** the project **be finished** on time?（项目会按时完成吗？）
- **Will** new employees **be hired** this year?（今年会雇新员工吗？）

#### 例句对比

<div class="example-wrong">❌ A new hospital will built next year.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ A new hospital <strong>will be built</strong> next year.（will 后面必须有 be！）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The report will be finish by Friday.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The report will be <strong>finished</strong> by Friday.（需要过去分词 finished，不是原形 finish）</div>

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作 Task 1** 预测未来趋势时可用将来时被动：

- *"It is predicted that the number **will be doubled** by 2030."*
- *"More resources **will be allocated** to education in the future."*

**雅思写作 Task 2** 提出建议时常用：

- *"Stricter laws **should be introduced** to protect the environment."*（更严格的法律应该被引入来保护环境。）
- *"More money **should be invested** in public transportation."*（更多的钱应该被投资在公共交通上。）

</div>

#### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="The exam results ___ (announce) next week."
  answer="will be announced"
  hint="next week = 将来 → will be + announced"
/>

<Quiz
  question="A new bridge ___ (not / build) until 2028."
  answer="will not be built"
  hint="将来时被动否定：will not (won't) be + built"
/>

### 3.4 现在进行时被动

**构成：am / is / are + being + 过去分词**

用于描述**此刻正在被做的事**。

- The road **is being repaired** at the moment.（这条路正在被修缮。）
- New employees **are being trained** this week.（新员工本周正在接受培训。）
- I **am being interviewed** right now.（我正在被面试。）

#### 否定句

- The car **is not being used** right now.（这辆车现在没有在被使用。）

#### 疑问句

- **Is** the building **being renovated**?（这栋楼正在被翻修吗？）

::: warning 中国人高频错误
进行时被动有**两个** be 的成分（is + being），很多人会漏掉 **being**：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The road is repaired now.（这是一般现在时被动，意思变成"路经常被修"）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The road <strong>is being repaired</strong> now.（正在被修 → 需要 being）</div>
:::

#### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="A new shopping mall ___ (build) near my house right now."
  answer="is being built"
  hint="right now = 正在进行 → is being + built"
/>

### 3.5 现在完成时被动

**构成：have / has + been + 过去分词**

用于描述**已经被做完的事**（强调结果或和现在的关联）。

| 主语 | 构成 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| I / You / We / They | have been + 过去分词 | The tickets **have been sold** out.（票已经卖光了。） |
| He / She / It | has been + 过去分词 | The report **has been finished**.（报告已经完成了。） |

#### 否定句

- The homework **has not (hasn't) been submitted** yet.（作业还没有被提交。）
- The documents **have not (haven't) been signed** yet.（文件还没有被签署。）

#### 疑问句

- **Has** the email **been sent**?（邮件发了吗？）
- **Have** the results **been published** yet?（结果公布了吗？）

::: warning 中国人高频错误
完成时被动有三个部分（have/has + been + 过去分词），很多人会漏掉 **been**：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The work has finished.（这变成了主动语态："工作完成了自己"，不通）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The work <strong>has been finished</strong>.（工作已经被完成了。）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Many changes have made in recent years.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Many changes <strong>have been made</strong> in recent years.（很多变化已经被做出。）</div>
:::

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作 Task 1 & Task 2** 现在完成时被动非常高频：

- *"Much progress **has been made** in the field of technology."*（技术领域已经取得了很大进步。）
- *"Several studies **have been conducted** on this topic."*（关于这个话题已经进行了多项研究。）
- *"The number of students **has been increasing** since 2010."*（学生人数自2010年以来一直在增长。）——这是现在完成进行时，注意区分
- *"It **has been argued** that..."*（有人认为...）

</div>

#### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="The bridge ___ (repair) recently."
  answer="has been repaired"
  hint="recently → 现在完成时被动：has been + repaired"
/>

<Quiz
  question="Over 1 million copies ___ (sell) since the book was published."
  answer="have been sold"
  hint="since... → 现在完成时。copies = 复数 → have been + sold"
/>

### 3.6 过去完成时被动

**构成：had been + 过去分词**

用于描述**过去某个时间点之前已经被做完的事**（"过去的过去"的被动）。

- By the time I arrived, the door **had been locked**.（我到的时候，门已经被锁了。）
- The letter **had been sent** before he changed his mind.（信在他改主意之前已经被寄出了。）
- All the food **had been eaten** by the time we got there.（我们到那里的时候，所有食物都已经被吃光了。）

#### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="By the time the police arrived, the car ___ (steal)."
  answer="had been stolen"
  hint="过去的过去 → had been + stolen（steal 的过去分词）"
/>

## 4. by + 动作执行者

### 什么时候用 by？

被动句中，原来的"执行者"（做动作的人/物）可以用 **by + 名词** 放在句末：

- *Hamlet* **was written** **by Shakespeare**.（《哈姆雷特》是莎士比亚写的。）
- The window **was broken** **by a football**.（窗户被一个足球砸碎了。）

### 什么时候省略 by？

**大多数被动句是不写 by 的！** 以下情况通常省略：

| 情况 | 例子 | 为什么省略 |
|------|------|-----------|
| **执行者不重要/不知道** | My bike **was stolen**.（我的自行车被偷了。） | 不知道是谁偷的 |
| **执行者是"人们"泛指** | English **is spoken** worldwide. | 不需要说 "by people" |
| **显而易见** | He **was arrested**.（他被逮捕了。） | 显然是警察逮捕的 |
| **正式/学术写作** | The experiment **was conducted** in 2020. | 学术写作中强调的是实验本身 |

### 什么时候保留 by？

| 情况 | 例子 | 为什么保留 |
|------|------|-----------|
| **执行者是重要的新信息** | This painting **was created by Picasso**. | 重点就是"毕加索画的" |
| **需要强调是谁做的** | The decision **was made by the CEO**. | 强调是CEO做的决定 |
| **有对比意义** | The book **was written by** a 12-year-old child. | 强调"才12岁就写了书" |

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**不要在不需要 by 的地方硬加 by！**

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Rice is grown by people in many Asian countries.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Rice is grown in many Asian countries.（"by people"多余，谁种的不重要）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The window was broken by someone.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The window was broken.（"by someone"太模糊，直接省略更好）</div>
:::

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作要点：** 学术写作中，大量被动句**不带 by**——这正是被动语态"客观""去个人化"的好处：

- *"The data **were collected** through online surveys."*（不用说 "by researchers"）
- *"It **can be seen** that the number increased."*（不用说 "by us"）
- *"The graph **shows** that..." （主动） vs "It **is shown** in the graph that..."（被动）* — 两种都可以在 Task 1 中使用

</div>

#### 练习题

<Quiz
  question="这句话需要 by 吗？选 A 或 B：The castle was built ___. A) by workers B) in the 15th century"
  answer="B"
  hint="城堡被谁建的不重要，重要的是什么时候建的 → 选 B: in the 15th century"

## 5. 被动语态的否定句和疑问句总结

### 否定句：在 be 后加 not

| 时态 | 否定形式 |
|------|---------|
| 一般现在时 | is **not** cleaned / are **not** cleaned |
| 一般过去时 | was **not** cleaned / were **not** cleaned |
| 一般将来时 | will **not** be cleaned |
| 现在进行时 | is **not** being cleaned |
| 现在完成时 | has **not** been cleaned / have **not** been cleaned |
| 过去完成时 | had **not** been cleaned |

### 疑问句：把第一个助动词提到主语前

| 时态 | 疑问形式 |
|------|---------|
| 一般现在时 | **Is** the room cleaned? |
| 一般过去时 | **Was** the room cleaned? |
| 一般将来时 | **Will** the room be cleaned? |
| 现在进行时 | **Is** the room being cleaned? |
| 现在完成时 | **Has** the room been cleaned? |
| 过去完成时 | **Had** the room been cleaned? |

## 6. 主动改被动的步骤

很多雅思题目会要求你把主动句改写成被动句。按照以下**三步法**操作：

### 第一步：找出主语、动词、宾语

> **主动句：** People **celebrate** Chinese New Year in many countries.
>
> - 主语（执行者）：People
> - 动词：celebrate
> - 宾语（承受者）：Chinese New Year

### 第二步：宾语变主语，动词变 be + 过去分词

> **Chinese New Year** → 新主语
>
> celebrate → **is celebrated**（一般现在时 → is + 过去分词）

### 第三步：原主语变 by 短语（可省略）

> by people → 泛指的"人们"，省略

### 最终结果

> **被动句：** Chinese New Year **is celebrated** in many countries.（春节在很多国家被庆祝。）

### 更多转换练习

| 主动句 | 被动句 |
|--------|--------|
| Alexander Bell **invented** the telephone. | The telephone **was invented** by Alexander Bell. |
| They **have repaired** the road. | The road **has been repaired**. |
| Someone **will deliver** the package tomorrow. | The package **will be delivered** tomorrow. |
| The teacher **is explaining** the lesson. | The lesson **is being explained** by the teacher. |

## 7. 综合对比表

| 时态 | 被动构成 | 例句 |
|------|---------|------|
| 一般现在时 | am/is/are + done | English **is spoken** here. |
| 一般过去时 | was/were + done | The house **was built** in 1900. |
| 一般将来时 | will be + done | The project **will be completed** soon. |
| 现在进行时 | am/is/are being + done | The car **is being repaired**. |
| 过去进行时 | was/were being + done | The road **was being repaired** when I passed. |
| 现在完成时 | have/has been + done | The email **has been sent**. |
| 过去完成时 | had been + done | The work **had been finished** before noon. |
| 将来完成时 | will have been + done | The bridge **will have been built** by 2030. |

> 记忆技巧：**所有被动语态的最后一个词永远是"过去分词"！** 前面的部分就是 be 动词随时态变化。
