# 常见句式错误 Common Sentence Errors

::: tip 错误是最好的老师
中国学生写英语时，最大的问题不是词汇量不够，而是**句子结构有问题**。很多错误源于用中文的思维方式写英语——直译中文、遗漏主语、语序混乱等。这些错误在雅思写作中会被直接扣分。本章列举中国考生最常见的 7 类句式错误，帮你逐一排查、逐一纠正。
:::

## 1. Run-on Sentences 粘连句

### 什么是 Run-on Sentence？

Run-on Sentence 是指**两个或以上完整的句子被不正确地连在一起**，没有使用正确的标点或连接词。这是英语写作中最常见的错误之一。

### 两种常见形式

#### 形式一：逗号拼接（Comma Splice）

用逗号连接两个完整句子，但没有连词：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The exam was difficult, many students failed.</div>
<div class="example-wrong">❌ I love this city, it has many beautiful parks.</div>
<div class="example-wrong">❌ She is my best friend, we met in high school.</div>

#### 形式二：无标点粘连（Fused Sentence）

两个完整句子之间没有任何标点或连词：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The exam was difficult many students failed.</div>
<div class="example-wrong">❌ I love this city it has many beautiful parks.</div>

### 四种修改方法

以 "The exam was difficult, many students failed." 为例：

| 方法 | 修改结果 | 适用场景 |
|------|---------|---------|
| 1. 分成两句 | The exam was difficult**.** Many students failed. | 最简单直接 |
| 2. 加并列连词 | The exam was difficult**, so** many students failed. | 表示因果关系 |
| 3. 用从属连词 | **Because** the exam was difficult, many students failed. | 复合句，更高级 |
| 4. 用分号 | The exam was difficult**;** many students failed. | 两句关系紧密 |

### 更多例句练习

| 错误 ❌ | 修改 ✅ |
|--------|--------|
| I was hungry, I ate lunch. | I was hungry**, so** I ate lunch. |
| She speaks French, she also speaks Spanish. | She speaks French**, and** she also speaks Spanish. |
| It was raining, we stayed home. | **Because** it was raining, we stayed home. |
| The movie was boring, I fell asleep. | The movie was **so** boring **that** I fell asleep. |

::: warning 中国人高频错误
中文用逗号非常自由，一个长句子可以用很多逗号一直连下去："今天天气很好，我们去公园了，公园里人很多，我们玩得很开心。"

英语**绝不允许**这样做！每个逗号分隔的部分如果是完整句子，就必须用连词或改成独立句子：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Today the weather was nice, we went to the park, there were many people, we had a great time.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Today the weather was nice, <strong>so</strong> we went to the park. There were many people, <strong>and</strong> we had a great time.</div>
:::

## 2. Sentence Fragments 残缺句

### 什么是 Sentence Fragment？

残缺句是一个**不完整的句子**——缺少主语、谓语，或者只是一个从句（没有主句）。虽然能表达一定意思，但语法上不成立。

### 三种常见形式

#### 形式一：缺少主语

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Is very important to learn English.（缺少主语）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>It</strong> is very important to learn English.（补上形式主语 it）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Was raining yesterday.（缺少主语）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>It</strong> was raining yesterday.（天气用 it 做主语）</div>

#### 形式二：缺少谓语动词

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The girl with long hair.（只有名词短语，没有谓语）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The girl with long hair <strong>is my sister</strong>.（补上谓语）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Many students in the library.（没有谓语）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>There are</strong> many students in the library.（用 There be 句型）</div>

#### 形式三：只有从句，没有主句

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Because I was tired.（只有原因从句，没有主句——所以怎样？）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Because I was tired, <strong>I went to bed early</strong>.（补上主句）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Although she studied hard.（只有让步从句——然后呢？）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Although she studied hard, <strong>she didn't pass the exam</strong>.（补上主句）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ When I was a child.（只有时间从句）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ When I was a child, <strong>I lived in a small town</strong>.（补上主句）</div>

### 判断是否是残缺句的方法

问自己三个问题：
1. **有主语吗？**（谁？什么？）
2. **有谓语动词吗？**（做了什么？怎么样？）
3. **意思完整吗？**（如果以 because, although, when 等开头，后面有主句吗？）

如果任何一个答案是"没有"，那就是残缺句。

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**中文可以省略主语，英语不行！**

中文经常省略主语："（我）去上学了"、"（外面）下雨了"、"（这）很重要"。英语中主语**不能省略**（祈使句除外）：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Is raining outside.（缺少 It）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>It</strong> is raining outside.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Very delicious!（缺少主语和谓语）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>It is</strong> very delicious! / <strong>The food is</strong> very delicious!</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Has many advantages.（缺少主语）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>It / This method</strong> has many advantages.</div>
:::

## 3. Subject-Verb Agreement Errors 主谓不一致

### 常见错误场景

主谓一致的完整规则已在 [五大基本句型](./01-basic-patterns.md) 中详细讲解。这里列出最高频的错误场景：

#### 错误一：第三人称单数忘加 -s

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She <strong>go</strong> to school every day.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She <strong>goes</strong> to school every day.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Technology <strong>play</strong> an important role.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Technology <strong>plays</strong> an important role.</div>

#### 错误二：被介词短语干扰

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The quality of the products <strong>are</strong> excellent.（误把 products 当主语）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The quality of the products <strong>is</strong> excellent.（主语是 quality，单数）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ One of my friends <strong>are</strong> a doctor.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ One of my friends <strong>is</strong> a doctor.（主语是 one，单数）</div>

#### 错误三：不可数名词用了复数谓语

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The information <strong>are</strong> incorrect.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The information <strong>is</strong> incorrect.（information 不可数，用单数）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The advice he gave me <strong>were</strong> very useful.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The advice he gave me <strong>was</strong> very useful.（advice 不可数）</div>

#### 错误四：the number of vs. a number of

| 表达 | 含义 | 谓语 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| **the** number of + 复数名词 | …的数量 | **单数** | The number of students **has** increased. |
| **a** number of + 复数名词 | 许多… | **复数** | A number of students **have** complained. |

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The number of cars <strong>have</strong> increased.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The number of cars <strong>has</strong> increased.（the number 是主语，单数）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ A number of people <strong>has</strong> arrived.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ A number of people <strong>have</strong> arrived.（a number of = many，复数）</div>

### 主谓不一致速查表

| 常见陷阱 | 正确谓语 | 记忆方法 |
|---------|---------|---------|
| everyone/somebody/nothing | 单数 | -one/-body/-thing = 单数 |
| news/information/advice | 单数 | 不可数 = 单数 |
| the number of... | 单数 | "数量"是一个数字 |
| a number of... | 复数 | = many |
| each/every + 名词 | 单数 | "每一个" = 单个 |
| 名词 + 介词短语 | 看名词 | 忽略介词短语 |
| neither A nor B | 跟 B | 就近原则 |

## 4. Dangling Modifiers 悬垂修饰语

### 什么是悬垂修饰语？

悬垂修饰语是指**修饰语（通常是分词短语或不定式短语）找不到它应该修饰的对象**，导致句子意思混乱甚至可笑。

### 问题所在

修饰语（如分词短语）默认修饰**紧跟其后的名词**。如果这个名词不是修饰语的逻辑主语，就会产生错误：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ <strong>Walking down the street</strong>, the trees were beautiful.
（字面意思：树在街上走？修饰语 "Walking down the street" 的逻辑主语应该是"我"，但句子主语是 the trees）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>Walking down the street</strong>, <strong>I</strong> saw beautiful trees.
（"我"在街上走时，看到了美丽的树。修饰语的逻辑主语是 I，正确。）</div>

### 更多例句

| 错误 ❌ | 分析 | 正确 ✅ |
|--------|------|--------|
| **Running to catch the bus**, my bag fell. | 包在跑步？ | **Running to catch the bus**, **I** dropped my bag. |
| **After studying all night**, the exam was easy. | 考试在学习？ | **After studying all night**, **I** found the exam easy. |
| **Born in 1990**, the company hired her. | 公司在1990年出生？ | **Born in 1990**, **she** was hired by the company. |
| **To improve your English**, practice is important. | 练习要提高英语？ | **To improve your English**, **you** should practice every day. |
| **Having finished dinner**, the TV was turned on. | 电视吃完了晚饭？ | **Having finished dinner**, **we** turned on the TV. |

### 修改原则

**修饰语（分词短语、不定式短语等）后面紧跟的主语必须是修饰语的逻辑主语。**

修改方法有两种：

| 方法 | 操作 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 方法一：改主句主语 | 让正确的主语跟在修饰语后面 | ~~Walking down the street, the trees were beautiful.~~ → **Walking down the street, I** noticed the beautiful trees. |
| 方法二：改修饰语为从句 | 把分词短语改成完整从句 | ~~Walking down the street, the trees were beautiful.~~ → **While I was walking down the street**, the trees were beautiful. |

::: warning 中国人高频错误
中文不要求分词短语的逻辑主语必须与句子主语一致，所以中国学生特别容易犯这个错误：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Considering the current situation, more efforts should be made.
（谁在 considering？efforts 在 considering？）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Considering the current situation, <strong>we</strong> should make more efforts.
（we 在 considering，正确。）</div>

**注意：** 有些分词短语已经变成了固定用法，不算悬垂修饰语：
- **Generally speaking**, English is not that difficult.（一般来说…）
- **Judging from** the evidence, he is guilty.（根据证据来看…）
- **Considering** his age, he runs very fast.（考虑到他的年龄…）

这些是公认的固定表达，雅思中可以使用。
:::

## 5. Parallel Structure 平行结构

### 什么是平行结构？

当句子中有**两个或以上并列的成分**时，它们在语法形式上必须保持一致。这就是平行结构（Parallel Structure）。

### 基本规则：形式一致

| 正确 ✅ | 为什么正确 |
|--------|-----------|
| I like **reading**, **writing**, and **speaking**. | 三个 -ing 形式 |
| She is **smart**, **hardworking**, and **kind**. | 三个形容词 |
| He wants **to study** abroad and **to work** in the UK. | 两个 to + 动词 |

| 错误 ❌ | 为什么错误 |
|--------|-----------|
| I like **reading**, **to write**, and **speak**. | 混用了 -ing、to do、原形 |
| She is **smart**, **hardworking**, and **has kindness**. | 混用了形容词和动词短语 |
| He wants **to study** abroad and **working** in the UK. | 混用了 to do 和 -ing |

### 需要注意平行结构的场景

#### 场景一：and / or / but 连接的并列成分

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She enjoys <strong>swimming, to cycle</strong>, and <strong>running</strong>.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She enjoys <strong>swimming, cycling</strong>, and <strong>running</strong>.（全部用 -ing）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The job requires <strong>experience, being creative</strong>, and <strong>dedication</strong>.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The job requires <strong>experience, creativity</strong>, and <strong>dedication</strong>.（全部用名词）</div>

#### 场景二：比较结构（than / as...as）

<div class="example-wrong">❌ <strong>Reading</strong> is more useful than <strong>to watch</strong> TV.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>Reading</strong> is more useful than <strong>watching</strong> TV.（比较的两项形式一致）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She is as good at <strong>singing</strong> as <strong>to dance</strong>.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She is as good at <strong>singing</strong> as <strong>dancing</strong>.</div>

#### 场景三：not only...but also / either...or / neither...nor

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She is not only <strong>beautiful</strong> but also <strong>has intelligence</strong>.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She is not only <strong>beautiful</strong> but also <strong>intelligent</strong>.（两个形容词）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ You can either <strong>stay here</strong> or <strong>going home</strong>.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ You can either <strong>stay here</strong> or <strong>go home</strong>.（两个动词原形）</div>

#### 场景四：列举（包括用冒号引出的列表）

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The report covers three areas: <strong>education, how to improve healthcare</strong>, and <strong>the economy</strong>.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The report covers three areas: <strong>education, healthcare</strong>, and <strong>the economy</strong>.（全部用名词）</div>

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作中平行结构非常重要！** 评分标准中 Grammatical Range and Accuracy 要求句子结构准确。平行结构错误会被视为语法错误，影响分数。

常见雅思写作场景：
- *"The government should **invest** in education, **improve** healthcare, and **create** more jobs."*（三个动词原形并列）✅
- *"This trend can be attributed to **economic growth, technological advancement**, and **social change**."*（三个名词短语并列）✅

</div>

## 6. 中式英语常见句式错误

### 什么是中式英语？

中式英语（Chinglish）是指**直接把中文的表达方式翻译成英语**，虽然可能能被理解，但不符合英语的表达习惯。在雅思考试中，这类错误会影响 **Lexical Resource** 和 **Grammatical Range** 两项评分。

### 错误一：主语缺失（中文省略主语的习惯）

中文经常省略主语，英语不行：

| 中文 | 中式英语 ❌ | 正确英语 ✅ |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| 很重要 | Is very important. | **It** is very important. |
| 如果可能的话 | If possible, can try again. | If possible, **you/we** can try again. |
| 有很多问题 | Have many problems. | **There are** many problems. / **We** have many problems. |

#### 特殊情况：It 做形式主语

中文说"学英语很重要"，直译是 "Learn English is important." 但英语中动词/动词短语不能直接做主语，需要用 **It** 做形式主语：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Learn English is very important.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>It is</strong> very important <strong>to learn</strong> English.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>Learning English</strong> is very important.（用 -ing 形式做主语也可以）</div>

### 错误二：动词重叠使用

中文一个句子可以有多个动词连用："我喜欢去超市买东西"。英语中一个简单句只能有**一个谓语动词**：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I like go to the supermarket buy things.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I like <strong>to go</strong> to the supermarket <strong>to buy</strong> things.（用不定式 to do）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I like <strong>going</strong> to the supermarket <strong>to buy</strong> things.（用 -ing 形式）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She decided go abroad study.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She decided <strong>to go</strong> abroad <strong>to study</strong>.</div>

### 错误三：because...so / although...but 同时使用

这是最经典的中式英语错误，在 [并列句与复合句](./02-compound-complex.md) 中已详细讲解：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Because he was tired, so he went to bed.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Because he was tired, he went to bed.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ He was tired, so he went to bed.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Although it rained, but we still went out.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Although it rained, we still went out.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ It rained, but we still went out.</div>

### 错误四：There be + have 混用

<div class="example-wrong">❌ There have many people in the park.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ There <strong>are</strong> many people in the park.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Our school there are 2000 students.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ There are 2000 students <strong>in our school</strong>.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Our school <strong>has</strong> 2000 students.</div>

### 错误五：直译中文固定搭配

| 中文 | 中式英语 ❌ | 正确英语 ✅ |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| 提高自己 | improve myself | improve **my skills / my English** |
| 开阔眼界 | open my eye | **broaden my horizons** |
| 给他打电话 | give him a telephone | **give him a call** / **call him** |
| 身体健康 | body health | **physical health** |
| 学习知识 | study knowledge | **acquire / gain knowledge** |
| 有好处 | have good | **be beneficial** / **be advantageous** |
| 随着社会的发展 | With the society develop | **With the development of society** |
| 越来越多的人 | More and more people（可用但太口语化） | **An increasing number of people** / **A growing number of people** |

### 错误六：形容词和名词混淆

中文里"重要"既可以做形容词也可以做名词，英语要区分 important（形容词）和 importance（名词）：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Education is great <strong>important</strong>.（important 是形容词，不能跟在 great 后面做名词）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Education is <strong>of great importance</strong>.（importance 是名词）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Education is <strong>very important</strong>.（important 是形容词，用 very 修饰）</div>

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**最容易直译出错的句型总结：**

| 中文句式 | 错误翻译 ❌ | 正确表达 ✅ |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| 我认为…不… | I think...not... | I **don't** think... |
| 虽然…但是… | Although...but... | Although...,（无 but） |
| 因为…所以… | Because...so... | Because...,（无 so） |
| 使我… | make me to do | make me **do**（无 to） |
| 让我… | let me to do | let me **do**（无 to） |
| …的人 | ...people | people **who**... |
| 有越来越多的人 | There are more and more people | **An increasing number of people** |
| 对…有好处 | have good to | **be good for** / **be beneficial to** |
:::

## 7. Word Order 语序问题

### 英语基本语序

英语是 **SVO 语序**（主语-谓语-宾语），与中文基本一致。但在修饰语的位置上，中英文有很大差异。

### 问题一：形容词顺序

当多个形容词修饰同一个名词时，英语有**固定的顺序**：

| 顺序 | 类别 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | 限定词 (determiner) | a, the, my, this |
| 2 | 观点/评价 (opinion) | beautiful, nice, terrible |
| 3 | 大小 (size) | big, small, tall, short |
| 4 | 形状 (shape) | round, square, flat |
| 5 | 新旧 (age) | old, new, young, ancient |
| 6 | 颜色 (color) | red, blue, green |
| 7 | 来源/国籍 (origin) | Chinese, American, French |
| 8 | 材质 (material) | wooden, plastic, cotton |
| 9 | 用途/类型 (purpose) | sleeping (bag), running (shoes) |

> **记忆口诀（英文首字母）**：**OSASCOMP** — Opinion, Size, Age, Shape, Color, Origin, Material, Purpose

#### 例句

| 正确顺序 | 分析 |
|---------|------|
| a **beautiful small old** Chinese vase | 评价 + 大小 + 新旧 + 来源 |
| a **big round red** ball | 大小 + 形状 + 颜色 |
| a **lovely little old** Italian coffee table | 评价 + 大小 + 新旧 + 来源 + 用途 |

<div class="example-wrong">❌ a Chinese beautiful old vase</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ a <strong>beautiful old Chinese</strong> vase（评价→新旧→来源）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ a red big ball</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ a <strong>big red</strong> ball（大小→颜色）</div>

::: info 实用建议
日常使用中，很少会同时用三个以上形容词。**记住最重要的规则：评价形容词在前（beautiful, nice），客观描述在后（big, old, red, Chinese）。** 如果不确定顺序，尽量少用多个形容词，分成两句说。
:::

### 问题二：副词位置

英语中副词的位置比中文灵活但有规律：

#### 频率副词的位置

频率副词（always, usually, often, sometimes, never, seldom）通常放在：

| 规则 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| be 动词**之后** | She is **always** on time. |
| 实义动词**之前** | I **usually** get up at 7. |
| 助动词和实义动词**之间** | He has **never** been to Japan. |

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She always is late.（频率副词应在 be 动词之后）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She is <strong>always</strong> late.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I get up usually at 7.（频率副词应在实义动词之前）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I <strong>usually</strong> get up at 7.</div>

#### 程度副词的位置

程度副词（very, really, quite, extremely, rather）放在它修饰的形容词/副词**之前**：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The movie was interesting very.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The movie was <strong>very</strong> interesting.</div>

#### 方式副词、地点副词、时间副词的顺序

当句末同时出现多个副词/状语时，顺序通常是：

**方式 → 地点 → 时间**（How → Where → When）

| 例句 | 分析 |
|------|------|
| She sang **beautifully** **at the concert** **last night**. | 方式 → 地点 → 时间 |
| He works **hard** **in the office** **every day**. | 方式 → 地点 → 时间 |
| They played **happily** **in the park** **this morning**. | 方式 → 地点 → 时间 |

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She last night at the concert sang beautifully.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She sang <strong>beautifully at the concert last night</strong>.（方式→地点→时间）</div>

### 问题三：否定转移

中文说"我认为他不对"，英语要把否定词放到主句：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I think he is <strong>not</strong> right.（虽然不算语法错误，但不地道）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I <strong>don't</strong> think he is right.（否定转移到主句，这是英语的习惯表达）</div>

类似的动词还有：believe, suppose, expect, imagine

| 中文 | 中式表达 | 地道表达 ✅ |
|------|---------|-----------|
| 我认为这不可能 | I think it is not possible. | I **don't think** it is possible. |
| 我觉得她不会来 | I believe she will not come. | I **don't believe** she will come. |
| 我想他不知道 | I suppose he doesn't know. | I **don't suppose** he knows. |

### 问题四：疑问句语序

英语疑问句需要**倒装**（把助动词提到主语前面），中文不需要：

| 中文 | 中式英语 ❌ | 正确英语 ✅ |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| 你是学生吗？ | You are a student? | **Are you** a student? |
| 你喜欢什么？ | You like what? | What **do you** like? |
| 她在哪里工作？ | She works where? | Where **does she** work? |
| 你什么时候到的？ | You arrived when? | When **did you** arrive? |

<div class="example-wrong">❌ You can speak English?（只加问号不倒装——口语中偶尔可以，但正式场合/写作不行）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>Can you</strong> speak English?</div>

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**特殊疑问句的语序公式：**

**疑问词 + 助动词 + 主语 + 动词原形？**

- **What** + **do** + **you** + **think**? ✅
- **Where** + **does** + **she** + **live**? ✅
- **How** + **did** + **they** + **solve** the problem? ✅

**但如果疑问词本身做主语，不需要倒装：**
- **Who** broke the window?（谁打破了窗户？——who 做主语，不倒装）
- **What** happened?（发生了什么？——what 做主语，不倒装）
:::

## 常见错误总结表

| 错误类型 | 典型表现 | 修改方法 |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Run-on sentence | 逗号拼接两个完整句子 | 加连词/分号/分成两句 |
| Sentence fragment | 缺主语/谓语/只有从句 | 补全缺失成分 |
| 主谓不一致 | 第三人称忘加 -s | 找准主语，忽略干扰 |
| 悬垂修饰语 | 分词短语修饰错误对象 | 让正确主语跟在修饰语后 |
| 平行结构 | 并列成分形式不一致 | 统一为相同语法形式 |
| 中式英语 | 直译中文句式 | 学习英语地道表达 |
| 语序错误 | 形容词/副词位置不对 | 掌握英语语序规则 |

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作检查清单——写完后逐条对照：**

1. **每个句子都有主语和谓语吗？** → 避免残缺句
2. **有没有用逗号连接两个完整句子？** → 避免 run-on sentence
3. **主语和谓语在数上一致吗？** → 第三人称单数加 -s 了吗？
4. **分词短语的逻辑主语和句子主语一致吗？** → 避免悬垂修饰语
5. **并列成分形式一致吗？** → 检查 and/or/but 连接的部分
6. **有没有 because...so / although...but？** → 只能用一个
7. **形容词/副词位置正确吗？** → 频率副词在 be 后、实义动词前

</div>
