# 并列句与复合句 Compound & Complex Sentences

::: tip 为什么要学复杂句？
用简单句写英语就像说话只用短句："我饿了。我去吃饭。饭很好吃。"——虽然没有语法错误，但显得幼稚、单调。在雅思写作中，**只用简单句最多拿 5.5 分**。如果你能把短句组合成长句——"Because I was hungry, I went to eat, and the food was delicious."——就能展示更高的语法水平，拿到 6.5 分甚至更高。本章教你如何把简单句"升级"成高分句子。
:::

## 1. 三种句子类型

英语句子按结构分为三种：

| 类型 | 英文 | 定义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| 简单句 | Simple Sentence | 只有**一个**主谓结构 | I study English. |
| 并列句 | Compound Sentence | **两个或多个**简单句用并列连词连接 | I study English, **and** she studies French. |
| 复合句 | Complex Sentence | 一个主句 + 一个或多个从句（用从属连词连接） | I study English **because** I need it for work. |

还有一种：

| 类型 | 英文 | 定义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| 并列复合句 | Compound-Complex Sentence | 包含并列结构 + 从句 | **Although** it was raining, I went out, **and** I got wet. |

### 图解对比

```
简单句：    [主句]
            I like coffee.

并列句：    [主句] + 连词 + [主句]
            I like coffee, but she likes tea.

复合句：    [主句] + [从句]
            I drink coffee because it keeps me awake.

并列复合句：[主句] + 连词 + [主句] + [从句]
            I drink coffee and she drinks tea because we both need energy.
```

## 2. 简单句 Simple Sentence

### 什么是简单句？

简单句只包含**一个主谓结构**（一个主语 + 一个谓语动词）。但简单句不一定"短"！

| 例句 | 分析 |
|------|------|
| She laughed. | 最短的简单句（SV） |
| I love music. | SVO |
| The tall young man with glasses standing near the door is my brother. | 虽然很长，但只有一个主谓：man is |

### 简单句可以有并列主语或并列谓语

| 情况 | 例句 | 仍是简单句？ |
|------|------|-----------|
| 并列主语 | **Tom and Jerry** are friends. | ✅ 是（一个谓语 are） |
| 并列谓语 | She **sang and danced**. | ✅ 是（一个主语 she） |
| 两个主谓 | She sang **and** he danced. | ❌ 不是，这是并列句 |

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**不要把两个简单句用逗号直接连接！** 这叫"逗号拼接"（comma splice），是英语中的严重语法错误。

<div class="example-wrong">❌ She is tired, she wants to sleep.（两个完整句子用逗号连接 = 错误）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She is tired, <strong>so</strong> she wants to sleep.（加并列连词 so）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ She is tired. She wants to sleep.（分成两个句子）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>Because</strong> she is tired, she wants to sleep.（用从属连词改成复合句）</div>
:::

## 3. 并列句 Compound Sentence

### 什么是并列句？

并列句由**两个或多个**地位平等的简单句（叫做"分句"）用**并列连词**连接而成。两个分句是"平等的"——谁也不依赖谁，都能独立成句。

### 结构公式

```
[简单句1], + 并列连词 + [简单句2].
```

> **注意逗号**：并列连词前面通常要加逗号（尤其是两个分句都较长时）。

### FANBOYS — 七个并列连词

**FANBOYS** 是七个最常用的并列连词的首字母缩写，是雅思必须掌握的：

| 连词 | 含义 | 功能 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| **F** — for | 因为 | 解释原因 | I stayed home, **for** it was raining.（我待在家，因为下雨了。） |
| **A** — and | 和、而且 | 添加信息 | I like tea, **and** she likes coffee.（我喜欢茶，她喜欢咖啡。） |
| **N** — nor | 也不 | 否定添加 | I don't drink, **nor** do I smoke.（我不喝酒，也不抽烟。） |
| **B** — but | 但是 | 表示转折 | I studied hard, **but** I failed the exam.（我学习很努力，但考试没通过。） |
| **O** — or | 或者 | 表示选择 | Hurry up, **or** you will be late.（快点，否则你会迟到。） |
| **Y** — yet | 然而 | 表示转折（比 but 正式） | He is rich, **yet** he is unhappy.（他很富有，但不开心。） |
| **S** — so | 所以 | 表示结果 | It was cold, **so** I wore a jacket.（天很冷，所以我穿了外套。） |

### FANBOYS 详细用法

#### and — 添加、递进

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 添加信息 | I bought a book, **and** I read it that night.（我买了一本书，当晚就读了。） |
| 顺序动作 | She opened the door **and** walked in.（她开了门走了进去。） |
| 递进 | He is smart, **and** he is also hardworking.（他聪明，而且也很努力。） |

#### but / yet — 转折

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| but（日常） | I like him, **but** I don't trust him.（我喜欢他，但不信任他。） |
| yet（正式） | The task was difficult, **yet** she completed it.（任务很难，但她完成了。） |

#### so — 结果

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 因果关系 | The traffic was heavy, **so** I arrived late.（交通拥堵，所以我迟到了。） |

#### or — 选择、否则

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 选择 | You can stay, **or** you can leave.（你可以留下，或者离开。） |
| 警告 | Study hard, **or** you will fail.（努力学习，否则你会不及格。） |

#### for — 原因（较正式）

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 解释原因 | She must be tired, **for** she has been working all day.（她一定累了，因为她工作了一整天。） |

> **注意**：for 做连词时比 because 更正式，且只能放在两个分句之间，不能放在句首。

#### nor — 否定添加

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 前面是否定句 | I can't swim, **nor** can I dive.（我不会游泳，也不会潜水。） |

> **注意**：nor 后面要用**倒装语序**（nor + 助动词 + 主语）。

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**1. 不要用 because...so... 连用！**

中文说"因为…所以…"是一套固定搭配。但英语中 because 和 so **不能同时出现**在一个句子里——因为一个是从属连词，一个是并列连词，用一个就够了。

<div class="example-wrong">❌ <strong>Because</strong> it rained, <strong>so</strong> I stayed home.（because 和 so 不能同时用）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>Because</strong> it rained, I stayed home.（用 because，去掉 so）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ It rained, <strong>so</strong> I stayed home.（用 so，去掉 because）</div>

**2. 不要用 although...but... 连用！**

同理，中文"虽然…但是…"在英语中不能同时出现。

<div class="example-wrong">❌ <strong>Although</strong> he is rich, <strong>but</strong> he is unhappy.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>Although</strong> he is rich, he is unhappy.（用 although，去掉 but）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ He is rich, <strong>but</strong> he is unhappy.（用 but，去掉 although）</div>
:::

### 并列句的标点规则

| 情况 | 标点 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 两个较长的分句 | 连词前加**逗号** | I love reading books, **and** she loves watching movies. |
| 两个很短的分句 | 逗号可省略 | I ran **and** she walked. |
| 三个或以上分句并列 | 每个分句后加逗号（Oxford comma） | I bought apples, oranges, **and** bananas. |

## 4. 复合句 Complex Sentence

### 什么是复合句？

复合句由一个**主句（main clause）**和一个或多个**从句（subordinate clause）**组成。从句不能独立存在，必须依附于主句。

### 结构公式

```
[主句] + 从属连词 + [从句].
或
从属连词 + [从句], + [主句].
```

> **当从句在句首时，从句后面要加逗号；从句在句末时，通常不加逗号。**

### 常见从属连词

#### 时间从句

| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| when | 当…时 | I was sleeping **when** the phone rang.（电话响时我在睡觉。） |
| while | 当…时（持续） | **While** I was cooking, he was reading.（我做饭时，他在看书。） |
| before | 在…之前 | Finish your homework **before** you go out.（出去之前先写完作业。） |
| after | 在…之后 | I felt better **after** I took the medicine.（吃了药之后我感觉好多了。） |
| since | 自从 | I have lived here **since** 2020.（我从2020年起就住在这里。） |
| until / till | 直到 | Wait here **until** I come back.（在这里等，直到我回来。） |
| as soon as | 一…就 | I'll call you **as soon as** I arrive.（我一到就给你打电话。） |
| by the time | 到…时候 | **By the time** I arrived, the movie had started.（我到时电影已经开始了。） |

#### 原因从句

| 连词 | 含义 | 正式程度 | 例句 |
|------|------|---------|------|
| because | 因为 | 最常用 | I stayed home **because** it was raining.（因为下雨我待在家。） |
| since | 既然 | 较正式 | **Since** you are tired, take a rest.（既然你累了，休息一下吧。） |
| as | 由于 | 较正式 | **As** it was late, we decided to leave.（由于很晚了，我们决定离开。） |

#### 条件从句

| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| if | 如果 | **If** it rains, I will stay home.（如果下雨，我就待在家。） |
| unless | 除非 | I won't go **unless** you come with me.（除非你陪我去，否则我不去。） |
| as long as | 只要 | You can borrow it **as long as** you return it.（只要你还就可以借。） |
| provided (that) | 假如 | I'll help you **provided** you help me too.（假如你也帮我的话。） |

#### 让步从句

| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| although / though | 虽然 | **Although** he is young, he is very mature.（虽然他年轻，但他很成熟。） |
| even though | 即使 | I went out **even though** it was raining.（即使下雨我也出去了。） |
| while | 尽管 | **While** I understand your point, I disagree.（尽管我理解你的观点，但我不同意。） |

#### 目的从句

| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| so that | 以便 | I studied hard **so that** I could pass the exam.（我努力学习以便通过考试。） |
| in order that | 为了 | She saved money **in order that** she could buy a car.（她存钱为了买车。） |

#### 结果从句

| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| so...that | 如此…以至于 | She was **so** tired **that** she fell asleep.（她太累了以至于睡着了。） |
| such...that | 如此…以至于 | It was **such** a good movie **that** I watched it twice.（电影太好了，我看了两遍。） |

### 从句位置与逗号规则

| 从句位置 | 逗号 | 例句 |
|---------|------|------|
| 从句在后 | 通常**不加**逗号 | I stayed home **because** it was raining. |
| 从句在前 | **加**逗号 | **Because** it was raining**,** I stayed home. |

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Because it was raining I stayed home.（从句在前，缺少逗号）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Because it was raining<strong>,</strong> I stayed home.</div>

## 5. 如何避免 Run-on Sentences

### 什么是 Run-on Sentence？

Run-on sentence 是指**两个或多个完整的句子**被不正确地连接在一起。最常见的两种形式：

#### 形式一：逗号拼接（Comma Splice）

两个完整句子只用逗号连接，缺少连词：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I am tired, I want to sleep.（逗号拼接）</div>

#### 形式二：无标点连接（Fused Sentence）

两个完整句子之间完全没有标点：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I am tired I want to sleep.（无标点连接）</div>

### 四种修改方法

| 方法 | 修改结果 |
|------|---------|
| 1. 加句号分成两句 | I am tired**.** I want to sleep. |
| 2. 加并列连词 | I am tired**, so** I want to sleep. |
| 3. 用从属连词 | I want to sleep **because** I am tired. |
| 4. 用分号 | I am tired**;** I want to sleep. |

## 6. 分号、冒号、破折号的使用

### 分号（Semicolon）`;`

分号连接两个**密切相关**的完整句子，不需要连词：

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 连接相关句子 | Some people love the city**;** others prefer the countryside.（有些人喜欢城市；其他人喜欢乡村。） |
| 与过渡词连用 | I studied hard**;** however, I still failed.（我努力学习了；然而，我还是失败了。） |

#### 常见过渡词（与分号搭配使用）

| 过渡词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|--------|------|------|
| however | 然而 | I tried**;** **however,** I failed. |
| therefore | 因此 | It rained**;** **therefore,** we stayed inside. |
| moreover | 此外 | He is smart**;** **moreover,** he is hardworking. |
| furthermore | 而且 | The price is high**;** **furthermore,** the quality is poor. |
| nevertheless | 尽管如此 | It was risky**;** **nevertheless,** she tried. |
| consequently | 结果 | He didn't study**;** **consequently,** he failed. |
| meanwhile | 与此同时 | I cooked**;** **meanwhile,** she cleaned. |
| instead | 相反 | He didn't complain**;** **instead,** he smiled. |
| in addition | 另外 | She is talented**;** **in addition,** she is modest. |
| on the other hand | 另一方面 | Cities are exciting**;** **on the other hand,** they are noisy. |

::: warning 中国人高频错误
**however, therefore, moreover 等词不是连词！** 不能用它们来连接两个句子，必须配合分号或句号使用。

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I studied hard, <strong>however</strong> I failed.（however 不是连词，不能用逗号连接）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I studied hard<strong>; however,</strong> I failed.（分号 + however + 逗号）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I studied hard<strong>. However,</strong> I failed.（句号 + However 大写开头 + 逗号）</div>
:::

### 冒号（Colon）`:`

冒号的作用是"引出"——后面跟解释、列举或说明：

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 引出解释 | There is one problem**:** we have no money.（有一个问题：我们没钱。） |
| 引出列表 | I need three things**:** a pen, a notebook, and an eraser.（我需要三样东西：一支笔、一本笔记本和一块橡皮。） |
| 引出引言 | As Shakespeare said**:** "To be or not to be."（正如莎士比亚所说："生存还是毁灭。"） |

> **注意**：冒号前面必须是一个**完整的句子**。

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I need: a pen, a notebook, and an eraser.（I need 不是完整句子）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I need three things: a pen, a notebook, and an eraser.</div>

### 破折号（Dash）`—`

破折号用来插入额外信息、解释或转折，比逗号更强调，比括号更随意：

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 插入解释 | My best friend **—** the one I met in college **—** is getting married.（我最好的朋友——我在大学认识的那个——要结婚了。） |
| 强调 | There is only one thing I want **—** freedom.（我只想要一样东西——自由。） |
| 话题转换 | I was about to leave **—** and then the phone rang.（我正要走——然后电话响了。） |

### 标点使用总结

| 标点 | 功能 | 前后要求 |
|------|------|---------|
| 逗号 `,` | 分隔从句、列举项目 | 不能单独连接两个完整句子 |
| 分号 `;` | 连接紧密相关的完整句子 | 前后都必须是完整句子 |
| 冒号 `:` | 引出解释/列表 | 前面必须是完整句子 |
| 破折号 `—` | 插入补充、强调 | 用法灵活 |

## 7. 雅思写作中的复杂句

### Band 5.5 vs Band 6.5 vs Band 7.5 的句子对比

**题目：** *Some people think that the government should spend money on public transport. To what extent do you agree?*

#### Band 5.5（全部简单句）

> The government should spend money on public transport. Public transport is important. Many people use it every day. It can reduce pollution. Cars cause a lot of pollution.

**问题**：全是短句，单调，没有展示语法能力。

#### Band 6.5（简单句 + 并列句 + 复合句混合）

> The government should invest in public transport **because** it is used by millions of people daily. **Although** cars are convenient, they cause significant air pollution, **so** improving bus and rail services could help reduce emissions. **Furthermore**, affordable public transport can benefit low-income families **who** rely on it for commuting.

**优点**：使用了 because（原因从句）、although（让步从句）、so（并列句）、who（定语从句），句式多样。

#### Band 7.5（灵活使用多种复杂结构）

> It is widely acknowledged **that** governments have a responsibility to invest in public transport, **given that** it serves as a lifeline for a significant proportion of the population. **While** private vehicles offer convenience, **the environmental costs they incur** — including air pollution and traffic congestion — **make** a compelling case for prioritizing public transit. **Not only would** enhanced public transport reduce carbon emissions, **but** it would **also** promote social equity **by** providing affordable mobility to those who need it most.

**优点**：使用了 that 从句、given that、while 让步、破折号插入、not only...but also 倒装、by doing 结构。

### 雅思写作常用复杂句式

| 句式 | 模板 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 原因状语从句 | ... because / since / as ... | Many students fail **because** they lack practice. |
| 让步状语从句 | Although / While ..., ... | **Although** technology has benefits, it also has drawbacks. |
| 条件状语从句 | If ..., ... | **If** governments invest in education, the economy will improve. |
| 定语从句 | ... who / which / that ... | People **who** live in cities face more pollution. |
| 名词性从句 | It is + adj. + that ... | **It is undeniable that** education plays a vital role. |
| 结果状语从句 | ... so + adj. + that ... | The problem is **so** serious **that** immediate action is needed. |

<div class="ielts-tip">

**雅思写作复杂句使用建议：**

1. **不是越长越好**——每段混合使用简单句和复杂句，保持节奏感
2. **不要为了复杂而复杂**——如果一个简单句能清楚表达意思，就不要硬凑复杂句
3. **每段至少 1-2 个复杂句**——展示你的语法能力
4. **掌握 3-4 种从句就够了**——because（原因）、although（让步）、if（条件）、who/which（定语）是最实用的
5. **连接词要多样化**——不要每句都用 because，可以换成 since, as, due to the fact that

**黄金比例**：一篇 250 字的雅思作文中，约 60% 复杂句 + 30% 简单句 + 10% 并列句。

</div>

## 三种句子类型总结对比

| 特征 | 简单句 | 并列句 | 复合句 |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| 主谓结构数量 | 1 个 | 2 个或以上 | 2 个或以上 |
| 分句关系 | — | 平等（独立） | 主从（从句依赖主句） |
| 连接方式 | — | FANBOYS 并列连词 | 从属连词 |
| 逗号规则 | — | 连词前加逗号 | 从句在前加逗号 |
| 雅思分数 | Band 5 | Band 5.5-6 | Band 6-7+ |
| 例句 | I study. | I study, and she works. | I study because I need it. |
