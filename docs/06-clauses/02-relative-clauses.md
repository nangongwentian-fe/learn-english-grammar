# 定语从句 Relative Clauses

::: tip 用一句话理解定语从句
中文里"**穿红衣服的**那个女孩"——"穿红衣服的"就是用来**修饰和限定**"那个女孩"的。英语用从句来实现同样的功能：The girl **who is wearing a red dress**... 这种**修饰名词的从句**就叫定语从句（也叫关系从句）。
:::

---

## 1. 关系代词：who / which / that / whose

定语从句需要一个**关系词**来连接主句和从句。最核心的四个关系代词是：

### 基本用法表

| 关系代词 | 修饰对象 | 在从句中做什么 | 例句 |
|---------|---------|--------------|------|
| **who** | 人 | 主语/宾语 | The man **who** lives next door is a teacher.（住在隔壁的那个人是老师。） |
| **whom** | 人 | 宾语（正式） | The man **whom** I met yesterday is a teacher.（我昨天见到的那个人是老师。） |
| **which** | 物/事 | 主语/宾语 | The book **which** I bought is interesting.（我买的那本书很有趣。） |
| **that** | 人或物 | 主语/宾语 | The man **that** lives next door... / The book **that** I bought... |
| **whose** | 人或物 | 表示所属（"谁的"） | The girl **whose** father is a doctor...（父亲是医生的那个女孩……） |

### who vs which vs that 怎么选？

| 先行词（被修饰的名词） | 可用的关系代词 | 推荐 |
|----------------------|--------------|------|
| **人** | who / whom / that | 优先用 **who** |
| **物/事** | which / that | 都可以，that 更口语化 |
| **人 + 物**（混合） | 只能用 **that** | The boy and his dog **that** were playing...（那个男孩和他的狗……） |

### who 做主语 vs 做宾语

**做主语**（从句中缺主语）：

<div class="example-right">✅ The woman <strong>who teaches us English</strong> is very kind.（教我们英语的那个女老师很和善。）</div>

分析：who = the woman，在从句中做主语（谁 teaches us? → who teaches us）

**做宾语**（从句中缺宾语）：

<div class="example-right">✅ The woman <strong>who(m) I met yesterday</strong> is very kind.（我昨天见到的那个女老师很和善。）</div>

分析：who(m) = the woman，在从句中做宾语（I met 谁? → I met who(m)）

### whose 的用法

**whose** 表示"**谁的**"，可以修饰人也可以修饰物：

<div class="example-right">✅ I know a girl <strong>whose mother is a famous singer</strong>.（我认识一个女孩，她妈妈是著名歌手。）</div>

<div class="example-right">✅ This is the house <strong>whose roof was damaged by the storm</strong>.（这是屋顶被暴风雨损坏的那栋房子。）</div>

::: warning 中国人高频错误
很多同学不会用 whose，改用很笨拙的写法：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ I know a girl. <strong>Her mother</strong> is a famous singer.（两个短句，不连贯）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ I know a girl <strong>whose mother</strong> is a famous singer.（一个复合句，更高级）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The student <strong>who his</strong> father is a doctor...（who 和 his 重复）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The student <strong>whose</strong> father is a doctor...</div>
:::

## 2. 限制性从句 vs 非限制性从句

这是定语从句最重要的分类，区别在于**有没有逗号**。

### 限制性定语从句（Defining / Restrictive）

**没有逗号**，从句提供**必要的信息**来识别是"哪一个"：

<div class="example-right">✅ Students <strong>who study hard</strong> usually get good grades.（努力学习的学生通常成绩好。）</div>

→ 不是所有学生，是**努力学习的那些**学生。从句是必不可少的，去掉就不知道在说哪些学生。

### 非限制性定语从句（Non-defining / Non-restrictive）

**有逗号**，从句提供**附加/补充信息**，去掉也不影响主句意思：

<div class="example-right">✅ My brother<strong>, who lives in London,</strong> is a doctor.（我哥哥住在伦敦，他是一名医生。）</div>

→ 我只有一个哥哥（已经明确是谁了），"住在伦敦"只是补充信息。

### 对比表

| | 限制性从句 | 非限制性从句 |
|---|-----------|------------|
| **逗号** | 没有逗号 | **有逗号** |
| **功能** | 限定"哪一个" | 补充额外信息 |
| **能否省略** | 不能省（省了不知道说谁） | 可以省（省了主句仍完整） |
| **that** | 可以用 that | **不能**用 that，只能用 who/which |
| **例句** | The man **who called you** is my uncle. | My uncle**,** **who lives in Beijing,** called you. |

### that 不能用于非限制性从句！

<div class="example-wrong">❌ My father<strong>, that</strong> is 60 years old, still works every day.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ My father<strong>, who</strong> is 60 years old, still works every day.（我父亲60岁了，仍然每天工作。）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ Beijing<strong>, that</strong> is the capital of China, has a long history.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ Beijing<strong>, which</strong> is the capital of China, has a long history.（北京是中国首都，有着悠久的历史。）</div>

::: warning 中国人高频错误
中文没有"限制性"和"非限制性"的区分，所以中国学生经常：
1. 该加逗号的不加：My mother who is a teacher... ❌（暗示你有多个母亲，只说做老师的那个）
2. 非限制性从句用 that：My mother, that is a teacher... ❌（非限制性从句不能用 that）

**判断技巧**：如果先行词是**专有名词**（人名、地名）或**独一无二的事物**（my mother, the sun），一般用**非限制性从句**（加逗号）。
:::

<div class="ielts-tip">

在雅思写作中，**非限制性定语从句**是展示语法多样性的利器：

- *"The internet**, which was invented in the 20th century,** has transformed how people communicate."*
- *"China**, which has the largest population in the world,** faces many environmental challenges."*
- *"This trend**, which is particularly noticeable among young people,** raises concerns about public health."*

每篇大作文用 1-2 个非限制性从句，就能有效提升语法得分。

</div>

## 3. where / when / why 引导的定语从句

除了关系代词，还有三个**关系副词**可以引导定语从句：

| 关系副词 | 先行词（被修饰的名词） | 在从句中做什么 | 等价于 |
|---------|---------------------|--------------|--------|
| **where** | 地点（place, city, country, house...） | 地点状语 | in/at which |
| **when** | 时间（time, day, year, moment...） | 时间状语 | on/in/at which |
| **why** | 原因（reason） | 原因状语 | for which |

### where（在那里）

<div class="example-right">✅ This is the city <strong>where I was born</strong>.（这是我出生的城市。）</div>

= This is the city **in which** I was born.

<div class="example-right">✅ I want to visit the school <strong>where my father studied</strong>.（我想参观我父亲上过的学校。）</div>

### when（在那时）

<div class="example-right">✅ I remember the day <strong>when we first met</strong>.（我记得我们第一次见面的那天。）</div>

= I remember the day **on which** we first met.

<div class="example-right">✅ Summer is the season <strong>when most people go on holiday</strong>.（夏天是大多数人度假的季节。）</div>

### why（为此原因）

<div class="example-right">✅ That is the reason <strong>why he left</strong>.（那就是他离开的原因。）</div>

= That is the reason **for which** he left.

::: warning 中国人高频错误
很多同学混淆 where 和 which：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ This is the city <strong>which</strong> I was born.（which 做宾语，但 born 后面缺介词 in）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ This is the city <strong>where</strong> I was born.（where = in which）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ This is the city <strong>in which</strong> I was born.（补上介词 in 也可以）</div>

**判断方法**：把关系词还原到从句中——
- I was born **in** the city. → 需要介词 → 用 **where** 或 **in which**
- I visited the city. → 不需要介词 → 用 **which/that**
:::

## 4. 介词 + which / whom

在正式写作中，可以把介词提到关系代词前面：

| 口语/非正式 | 正式/雅思写作 |
|-----------|------------|
| The city **which** I live **in** | The city **in which** I live |
| The man **who** I spoke **to** | The man **to whom** I spoke |
| The topic **which** we talked **about** | The topic **about which** we talked |
| The company **which** she works **for** | The company **for which** she works |

::: warning 注意
介词提前后，**只能用 which/whom**，不能用 that 或 who：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The city <strong>in that</strong> I live...</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The city <strong>in which</strong> I live...</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The man <strong>to who</strong> I spoke...</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The man <strong>to whom</strong> I spoke...</div>
:::

<div class="ielts-tip">

**介词 + which/whom** 结构在雅思写作中是加分句式：

- *"Education is a process **through which** people acquire knowledge and skills."*（教育是人们获得知识和技能的过程。）
- *"The extent **to which** technology affects daily life is remarkable."*（科技影响日常生活的程度是惊人的。）
- *"There are many reasons **for which** people choose to emigrate."*（人们选择移民有很多原因。）

</div>

## 5. 省略关系代词的情况

在口语和非正式写作中，关系代词可以省略，但有条件：

### 什么时候可以省略？

**当关系代词在从句中做宾语时，可以省略。**

| 完整写法 | 省略写法 | 可以省吗？ |
|---------|---------|----------|
| The book **which** I bought... | The book ~~which~~ I bought... | ✅ which 做宾语（I bought it） |
| The man **who** I met... | The man ~~who~~ I met... | ✅ who 做宾语（I met him） |
| The movie **that** we watched... | The movie ~~that~~ we watched... | ✅ that 做宾语（we watched it） |

### 什么时候不能省略？

**当关系代词在从句中做主语时，不能省略。**

| 完整写法 | 能省吗？ | 原因 |
|---------|---------|------|
| The man **who** lives next door... | ❌ 不能省 | who 做主语（谁 lives? → who lives） |
| The book **which** is on the table... | ❌ 不能省 | which 做主语（什么 is? → which is） |

### 简单判断法

看关系代词后面**紧跟的是什么**：
- 紧跟**动词** → 关系代词做主语 → **不能省略**：The man **who lives**...
- 紧跟**主语（名词/代词）** → 关系代词做宾语 → **可以省略**：The man (who) **I met**...

## 6. 中国学生最常见的定语从句错误

### 错误一：多余的代词

这是中国学生**最标志性的错误**！中文里"我买的**那本书**"翻译成英语时，很多同学会多加一个代词：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The book which I bought <strong>it</strong> is interesting.（多了 it！which 已经代替了 it）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The book which I bought is interesting.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The man who I met <strong>him</strong> yesterday is a teacher.（多了 him！）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The man who I met yesterday is a teacher.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The city where I was born <strong>there</strong> is very beautiful.（多了 there！）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The city where I was born is very beautiful.</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The girl whose father is a doctor, <strong>she</strong> is my classmate.（多了 she！）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The girl whose father is a doctor is my classmate.</div>

::: danger 为什么会犯这个错？
因为中文的定语从句结构是这样的：

"我买的**那本书**很有趣" → 中文里"那本书"必须出现

但英语里，关系代词（who/which/that）**已经替代了**那个名词，所以不能再重复：

The book **which（= the book）** I bought ~~it~~ is interesting.

**记住：关系代词 = 被修饰的名词，不需要再用代词重复！**
:::

### 错误二：用 what 代替 that/which

中文里"我需要的东西"可以翻译成 what I need（名词性从句）或 the thing that I need（定语从句），但很多同学在定语从句里误用 what：

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The thing <strong>what</strong> I need is a computer.</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The thing <strong>that</strong> I need is a computer.（已有先行词 the thing，用 that）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ <strong>What</strong> I need is a computer.（没有先行词，用 what 引导名词性从句）</div>

**规则：有先行词 → 用 that/which；没有先行词 → 用 what**

### 错误三：which 和 where 混淆

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The school <strong>where</strong> I visited last week is very large.（visited 后面缺宾语，不能用 where）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The school <strong>which</strong> I visited last week is very large.（which 做 visited 的宾语）</div>

<div class="example-wrong">❌ The school <strong>which</strong> I study is very large.（study 后面缺地点状语，应该是 study at）</div>
<div class="example-right">✅ The school <strong>where</strong> I study is very large.（where = at which）</div>

## 定语从句总结

| 项目 | 要点 |
|------|------|
| **修饰人** | who（主语/宾语）, whom（宾语，正式）, whose（所有格） |
| **修饰物** | which, that, whose |
| **修饰地点** | where (= in/at which) |
| **修饰时间** | when (= in/on/at which) |
| **修饰原因** | why (= for which) |
| **限制性** | 无逗号，不可省从句；可用 that |
| **非限制性** | 有逗号，可省从句；**不能**用 that |
| **省略关系词** | 关系词做宾语时可省略 |
| **介词提前** | 只能 + which/whom，不能 + that/who |

### 关系词选择流程图

1. 先行词是**人**？→ who/whom/whose/that
2. 先行词是**物**？→ which/that/whose
3. 先行词是**地点**且从句缺状语？→ where
4. 先行词是**时间**且从句缺状语？→ when
5. 先行词是 **reason** 且从句缺原因状语？→ why
6. **有逗号**（非限制性）？→ 不能用 that
7. **介词提前**？→ 只能用 which/whom
